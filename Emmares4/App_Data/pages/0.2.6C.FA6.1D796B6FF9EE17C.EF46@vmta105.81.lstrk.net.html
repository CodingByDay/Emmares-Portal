Medio Tiempo is becoming ever-so-popular throughout the entire industry. If you're unfamiliar with the Medio Tiempo, it's the highest part of a tobacco leaf that produces a plethora of concentrated oils. Being one of the thickest parts of the plant, Medio Tiempo is not as rare as the other parts of the tobacco leaf.
