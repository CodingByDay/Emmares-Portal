Hi there, 

 

When we published our report on disinformation, we titled it Memes That Kill: The Future Of Information Warfare. In it, we cover everything from deepfakes to bot armies. 

 

As dystopian as it might sound, that “future” is now. 

 

Nearly half of the people talking about Covid-19 on Twitter may be bots, according to Carnegie Mellon research. Conspiracy theories regarding cures are rife in Facebook groups. These forms of online discourse have fueled real-life action, often with destructive consequences, ranging from vandalism of 5G towers to hate crimes against Asian Americans to deaths from self-medication.
