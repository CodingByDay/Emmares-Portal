Invest in Your Own Growth
Once you've landed a job and settled into your role, you might think it's time to kick back and relax. Not so fast. You should continue to look for opportunities to grow in your career all the time.

There are so many ways to do this. You can help out on a project with a different department to get a better understanding of their work, learn new skills, and bring back insights to your own team. You might help out a busy colleague or take the initiative to propose a way to make an existing process easier. You could volunteer to learn a new system and train your co-workers or find another way to take on more responsibility.

When you challenge yourself, you're developing the knowledge (and reputation) that'll help get you to the next step of your career journey.
