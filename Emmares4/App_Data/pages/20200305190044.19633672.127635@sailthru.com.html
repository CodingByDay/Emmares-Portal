With coronavirus developments unfolding so quickly, you're probably hearing a lot of advice about how to react—guidelines for everything from prepping your pantry to washing your hands properly to avoiding touching your face.

We here at WIRED have been busy supplementing our extensive coronavirus coverage with practical advice about how to persevere in these times of fear and uncertainty. For example, we have a guide that outlines the best practices for working from home, in case your company asks you to relocate to your dining room for a few weeks. Also, your electronics are filthy, and you should learn how to scrub them clean, so we have a guide for that. You should also be careful of profiteering and companies making false health claims when buying things on Amazon. Even if your area isn't affected, this is all still good advice that can improve your health and happiness.
