Fascinating: the hackers are so bad at writing ransomware decryption tools for their own malware that it has created a market for third party decryption tools that are faster and more stable.

Of note: it’s still currently impossible to decrypt ransomware without the key provided by the hackers. These third party tools extract the decryption key to use. You’ll probably want to negotiate when dealing with ransomware hackers though.
