THE LATEST
Julian Shaw Walks On The Wild Side With Use Me
He tackled difficult subject matter with his previous docos Cup Of Dreams and Darling!: The Pieter-Dirk Uys Story, but Julian Shaw now truly goes dark with Use Me, an inside look at the dangerous world of fetishism and sexual obsession starring real life online humiliiatrix Ceara Lynch. 

The Alliance Francaise Film Festival

On the eve of his fourth and final fling as director of The Alliance Francaise French Film Festival, Philippe Platel runs through his list of highlights.
Kristofer Hivju: Running Downhill
Norwegian Game Of Thrones scene-stealer Kristofer Hivju is at it again in Downhill, pinching the film from Will Ferrell and Julia Louis-Dreyfus. 

Classic 10BA Aussie Rip Offs

From the sci-fi of The Time Guardians to the T&A excess of Melvin Son Of Alvin, here's a bunch of 10BA era flicks that ripped off the best of the US.
Miranda Otto: Downhill & Sabrina
The hard working Aussie actress chats about her latest  projects: the comedy Downhill and the Netflix series Chilling Adventures Of Sabrina. 

Berlin Film Festival Winners

The 2020 Berlin International Film Festival wrapped up for another year with the announcement of the eclectic winners of the Golden Bear Awards.
Stateless: Aussie TV At Its Best 
Cate Blanchett, Tony Ayres and Elise McCredie - co-creators of the powerful ABC-TV drama Stateless - talk about the creation of this incredible show.
