New Skift Research — EU Traveler Profile and Key Statistics: The Impact of COVID-19

As the world’s largest destination and source region for the global tourism economy, Europe has been the hardest hit by the pandemic. The COVID-19 impact on the tourism industry is especially grim for the European Union, a bloc heavily relying on free moments of member states but needed to close national borders.

Skift Research’s newly released EU Traveler Profile and Key Statistics: The Impact of COVID-19 report looks at the pre-COVID travel patterns of EU residents, and how the pandemic has changed these patterns. We highlight some segments with better-than-expected performance and analyze what 2021 has in store for the bloc.

What You’ll Learn From This Report

  • The Importance of European Tourism as a Destination, Source Market, and Economic Driver
  • European Travel Patterns Pre-COVID
  • Impact of COVID-19 on Travel Behavior
  • What Will 2021 Bring?
