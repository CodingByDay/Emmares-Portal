MY WAY OF MAKING NEW YEAR'S RESOLUTIONS

Knock, knock! A new year is knocking at the door! Have you made a list of things you would like to do in the coming year? Maybe you just want some peace ands quiet for yourself somewhere outdoors. Or get excited for the Summer Olympics! Whatever you decide, make sure visiting Slovenia is on your list.
