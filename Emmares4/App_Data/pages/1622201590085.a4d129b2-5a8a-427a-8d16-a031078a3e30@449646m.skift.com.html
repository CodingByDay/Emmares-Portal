Editor's Note

As we approach a long holiday weekend here in the U.S. and the hopeful start of summer for travel, it's important we all be mindful that the pandemic is far from over for many across the globe. We know a full travel recovery can't occur until vaccine distribution is far more equitable than it is today — so let's not lose sight of that goal.

With that, we are happy to offer you a special newsletter today highlighting some of our best coverage to prepare you for the summer ahead in travel, including some deep dives, our latest recovery index from our colleagues at Skift Research, and recent analysis about prospects for the summer.

We won't have our newsletters for you this coming Monday, but Skift will be back in your inbox on Tuesday morning.

From all of us here at Skift, we wish you a peaceful holiday weekend if you're celebrating, and a summer of full of renewed optimism.

— Tom Lowry, Editor-in-Chief
