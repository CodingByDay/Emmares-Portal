Welcome to Fantasy Football Yesterday week! All week long on CBS Sports HQ, the website, and the podcast, the Fantasy Football Today team will be turning back the clocks on Fantasy Football history since the start of CBS Sports Fantasy (SportsLine) in 1997. While I'm very excited to unveil our positional Mount Rushmores, reminisce on the biggest one year wonders, and reflect on the worst busts of all time, today we're kicking off the week with an Ultimate Fantasy Draft. For some reason, Ladainian Tomlinson's incredible 483-point 2006 season fell to #2 in the draft behind Marshall Faulk's 2000 season. And drafting in the seventh slot, I was thrilled to snag Randy Moss's 23 touchdown 2007 season with my first pick before taking Arian Foster in round 2.

We'll be discussing these picks and more from the Ultimate Fantasy Draft on our CBS Sports HQ show at noon ET, as well as on today's podcast. Other debates include Patrick Mahomes vs. Peyton Manning, Muhsin Muhammad vs. Andre Johnson, and Adrian Peterson's 2,097-yard season vs. Charlie Garner's 91-catch year.
