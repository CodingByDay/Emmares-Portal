Classic Cars Hot 30
Inside the October issue we reveal our famous HOT 30 – the 30 most compelling classic buys in today’s market, tipped by six experts – and reminisce about the days when they were cheap second-hand cast-offs.

We also facilitate one lucky reader’s dream drive in a Bentley ‘Blower’, track-test one of the original Lotus Elan 26R Goldbugs, tell the wild life story of Beatle George Harrison’s old Mercedes 600, explore a restoration of a Chevron B38 that walked a tightrope between preserving originality and making it more competitive, revisit the Citroën BX in its modern role as a rare survivor, and show you how to buy a BMW Z4 without regret…

All that – plus Quentin Willson and John Fitzpatrick, market news and events highlights, the monthly highs and lows of our own classics, four cars for sale tested, and much more – makes the October issue unmissable.
