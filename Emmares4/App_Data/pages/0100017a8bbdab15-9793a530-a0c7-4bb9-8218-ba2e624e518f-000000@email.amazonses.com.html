Speaking of keeping cool, here’s your water cooler trivia for the day. The first Wimbledon tennis tournament began on this day in 1877. Can’t get enough Wimbledon trivia? Here are a few to tide you over. On this day in 2000, Venus Williams won her first Wimbledon tournament. The tournament is one of four Grand Slam high-profile tennis events but the only one played on grass. The players wear all white both out of tradition and because the tournament has a strict dress code. And tradition is something Wimbledon has a lot of as the oldest tennis tournament in the world.
