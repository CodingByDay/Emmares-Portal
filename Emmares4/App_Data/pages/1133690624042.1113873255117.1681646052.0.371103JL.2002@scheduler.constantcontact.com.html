Revel in That CIRE Shine
Looking back, 2019 has been a year of abundance for CIRE Travel. New members have joined our team. We've added two locations in Washington D.C. and Kennebunkport, ME. Our refreshed editorial-style website launched. Familiarization trips ended with new and renewed relationships with our hotel, airline and destination travel partners. A CIRE agent won a coveted 30Under30 award. And, we hosted a slew of events, such as charity dinners, travel talks and lunch & learns.
 
Looking ahead, 2020 is just as bright. More travel to destinations around the world. France, New Zealand and Morocco, to name a few, are on the horizon. Eric will be a contributor to BostonMan magazine. The Kennebunkport location will host a travel talk on India. There's a distinct possibility CIRE will expand to Dubai. Where next? It's a big world.
 
We look forward to sharing our abundant expertise and concierge-level service with you year after year. After all, we're honored you trust us with your travel planning, and it's that trust that has brought us this far.
 
Wherever you go, whatever you do... Shine on, CIRE Travelers... Shine on.
