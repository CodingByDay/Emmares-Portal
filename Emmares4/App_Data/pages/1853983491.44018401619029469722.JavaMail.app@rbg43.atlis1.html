Dear Reader,

The use of psychedelics as an alternative to traditional psychiatric medications, such as selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs), is growing, based on evidence accrued through clinical trials. A recent example is a trial that showed that psilocybin is comparable in effectiveness to escitalopram, a commonly used antidepressant.
