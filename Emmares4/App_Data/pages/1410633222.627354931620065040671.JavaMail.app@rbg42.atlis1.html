May 03, 2021

Dear Reader,

Last week the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention released new guidance on outdoor mask wearing for vaccinated and unvaccinated people. The guidelines recognize the low risk of outdoor transmission, but critics say they are somewhat confusing. Also, a new study piles on more evidence that Black, Hispanic and Asian people are exposed to disproportionately more air pollution of almost every kind. And, how the flu season has all but disappeared.

Tanya Lewis, Senior Editor, Health & Medicine
