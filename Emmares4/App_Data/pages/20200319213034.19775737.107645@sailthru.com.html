There's one and only one topic on everyone's mind this week, and indeed that is the global response to the spread of the coronavirus. As the efforts to contain the outbreak pick up steam, WIRED will continue delivering breaking news reporting, analysis, and advice.

At Gadget Lab, we've been talking to experts, gathering helpful tips from colleagues, and offering guidance for those stuck in their homes for the coming weeks. While we should note that not everyone can work from home, those of us who do have that luxury might still need help keeping our houses in order. This week, we tell you what to stock up on and what to avoid, while making sure not to panic hoard. (You know who you are.) We also have a cleaning guide with suggestions on what to clean and how often to clean it, plus tips for effective sanitization. Also, we offer some support for parents who suddenly have to handle child care while logging in to their jobs.

Meanwhile, the consumer electronics world chugs along. Apple released some new hardware this week, we reviewed the Oculus Rift S, and Sonos proposed an awkward solution to its awkward sunsetting of older speakers.
