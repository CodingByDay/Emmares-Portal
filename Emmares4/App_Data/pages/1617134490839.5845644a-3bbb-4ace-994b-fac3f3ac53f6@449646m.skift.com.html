Share your thoughts in Skift’s 2021 State of Hospitality Survey!

The travel slowdown caused by Covid-19 forced hospitality businesses around the world to make tough decisions about how to serve guests, protect staff, and stay afloat. But it was also a year of radical change and innovation, where hospitality leaders discovered new ways to make their organizations more resilient, innovative, and ready to evolve their business for the future.

What comes next? Skift is calling all hospitality leaders to share their thoughts about emerging strategies, tools, technologies, and selling techniques they used to survive 2020, along with how they are positioning their businesses to capitalize on the recovery to come and succeed in the future. The results will be released in a report publishing later this summer.
