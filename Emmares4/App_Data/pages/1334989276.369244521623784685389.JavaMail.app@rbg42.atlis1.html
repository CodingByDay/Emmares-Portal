Dear Reader,

If I start feeling sleepy during the work day, I take a coffee break. But one day, I might swap the cup of joe for a handheld device that zaps my neck with electricity. A new study looked at Air Force members pulling an all nighter, giving some of them electric stimulation to the vagus nerve while others received placebos. The buzzed group was less tired and performed better on attention tests. 

Sophie Bushwick, Associate Editor, Technology
