We’re publishing a new trend report, in partnership with Seera Group: Decoding the Saudi Arabian Travel and Tourism Industry, which provides insights into how the Kingdom’s travel industry and Saudi Arabian travelers are evolving and transforming.

Saudi Arabia has been making significant moves to evolve its inbound and outbound travel. The Kingdom is improving its infrastructure, encouraging private investment to provide travelers with more places to visit, creating entertainment options, and relaxing rules that previously made Saudi Arabia impermeable. The outbound travel market is already quite mature — Saudi Arabians travel all over the world on a frequent basis and are high spenders.

These evolutions present an opportunity for travel brands around the world. SkiftX partnered with Seera Group to unearth some of the top trends, challenges, and growth opportunities in the region.

This report explores:

  • The current state of the travel industry in Saudi Arabia
  • The triggers for growth in the Kingdom’s travel industry
  • The challenges that Saudi Arabia faces in driving tourism
  • Understanding the nuanced needs of Saudi Arabia’s outbound travelers
  • Saudi Arabia’s plan to grow domestic and international inbound travel beyond pilgrim and business trips
