Nailing the Post-Vacation Rebound
Making the switch back from vacation mode to work mode can be tough. It's hard to hang on to the memories of lazy beach naps and sightseeing adventures now that you have to refocus on emails and deadlines.

As hard as it is, returning from vacation doesn't have to be so dreadful. Ease your way back into work by completing small tasks on your to-do list.

And remember, you can still take time for yourself even though vacation is over. Pick up a treat from a nearby coffee shop, plan a lunch, or just take a short walk. It may not be the beach but it could help you power through the rest of the day!
