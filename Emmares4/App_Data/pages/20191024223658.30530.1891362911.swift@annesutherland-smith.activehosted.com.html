I look forward to helping you to plan your trip to Japan - it is an incredible destination!

I would also like to invite you to join my Japan Travel Planning Facebook Group - the group provides some great options to ask questions to help you plan your trip and interact with other planning their trip and those who have recently returned. I also regularly share a broad range of articles about Japan - from the basics through to the inspirational, and both on and off the beaten track options.


If you have any questions or thoughts feel free to reach out at any time.
