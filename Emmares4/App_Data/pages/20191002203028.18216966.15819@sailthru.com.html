Today’s Headlines

Microsoft unveils new Surface, foldable phone, and more

Today was Microsoft's turn to show off some new products, and it didn't disappoint: a new Surface X (a thinner, sleeker Surface that runs Windows 10), Surface Earbuds (Microsoft's response to Apple's AirPods), and yes, a new foldable device called the Microsoft Surface Duo.

Tesla's new "Smart Summon" will fetch your car

Recently in a software update, Tesla updated its cars with a Smart Summon feature that allows drivers to call their Tesla to come to them. If you use it in an empty parking lot or a private driveway, it works pretty well. But in a crowded lot or areas where there's lots of grass? Not so well.
