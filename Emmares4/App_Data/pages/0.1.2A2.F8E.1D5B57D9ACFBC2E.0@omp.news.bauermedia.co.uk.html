The Last Call For Postage

December 25th is nearing, and there are just a few days left to ensure your gifts arrive in time. To save you from coming up with last-minute excuses or dashes to the shops, here’s a rundown of the last postage dates for Royal Mail and Amazon:

Royal Mail: The last day for First Class postage is December 20th and December 23rd for Special Delivery. The last postage date for Second Class has been and gone!

Amazon: The last day for Free Standard Delivery is December 20th, and paid Standard Delivery ends at 9.30am on December 22nd. If you’re a Prime member (or willing to pay a little extra for postage), One Day Delivery and Prime Now 2-Hour ends on December 23rd – you’ll receive your delivery on Christmas Eve, just in the nick of time.
