Cigar Reviews

The Aganorsa Lunatic JR 50th is hand-rolled in Nicaragua at Tabacos Valle de Jalapa S.A., Aganorsa's main facility. The filler and binder are constructed of the legendary, sweet, and smooth Aganorsa tobacco, grown on Aganorsa's own farms in Nicaragua. The wrapper is a silky-smooth Ecuadorian Habano leaf. The cigar also features a fringed foot, exposing portions of the binder and filler on the end. Make sure to pick up yours today at JRCigars.com.
