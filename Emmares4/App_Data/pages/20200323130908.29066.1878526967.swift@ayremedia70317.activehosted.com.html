The future is now
 

The Story

Observers of the gaming industry have long predicted that the future of gaming and gambling would be digital, global, and online. Real world, brick-and-mortar gambling has become positively outlawed, at least for the moment as COVID-19 rages on. And while the opportunity presents itself, we don’t have yet the legal tools to effectively manage it. We are lacking, and badly need, the policies and statutes that the operators and the customers both must have in order to make online interactive gaming a worthwhile enterprise.
