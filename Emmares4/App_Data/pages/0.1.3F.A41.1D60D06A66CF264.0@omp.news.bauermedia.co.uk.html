DISNEY+ HAS LANDED.

With the UK in a self-isolation lockdown, there’s never been a greater need for movies – magical favourites and barnstorming blockbusters to keep us entertained, inspired and distracted in this unsettling time. Perfect timing, then, for the launch of Disney+, a streaming service crammed with Disney animated classics, near-every chapter in the Star Wars saga, the majority of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, a huge catalogue of Pixar pics, and much more.

As well as firm film favourites, there are must-see Disney+ originals too – from the first ever live-action Star Wars TV show, The Mandalorian, to the return of Star Wars: The Clone Wars, plus original movies like the reimagined Lady And The Tramp, and Willem Dafoe-starring dogsled adventure Togo. Oh, and someone finally gave Jeff Goldblum a show where he just goes around, well, being Jeff Goldblum. It’s called – you guessed it – The World According To Jeff Goldblum. All that, and you can revisit the iconic X-Men: Animated Series, or delve back into 30 seasons of The Simpsons. Not sure where to start? Read Empire’s list of 23 things to watch on Disney+.

If you fancy signing up to Disney+, you can get a 7 day free trial and stream whatever takes your fancy. Once the trial is up, it costs £5.99 a month, or £59.99 for a one-year membership. It’s a small price to pay for some much-needed Disney magic.
