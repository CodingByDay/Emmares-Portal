New Skift Research — U.S. Digital Nomad Market Analysis 2021

Digital nomadism is breaking out into the mainstream after years of being available only to a few career paths. This trend is poised to generate hundreds of millions of dollars for the travel industry.

In Skift Research’s new U.S. Digital Nomad Market Analysis 2021 report, we examine current trends in remote work and what it means for the travel industry by using the U.S. as a case study of quantifying the potential addressable market. Even if we only focus on home renters who are likely to become digital nomads, we estimate that they could potentially add nearly $1 billion to room stay revenues.

What you'll Learn From This Report: 

  • How many Americans are currently working remotely and their prospects for returning to the office

  • Reasons for the rise of digital nomadism

  • Estimated U.S. Digital Nomad market size, population, and room nights

  • Recommendations for building travel businesses around digital nomads
