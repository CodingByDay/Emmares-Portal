With The AACTA Awards rolling out last night (check out our report below), why not continue the local love by checking out an Aussie flick this week? There's the comedy drama Standing Up For Sunny (starring R.J Mitte from TV's Breaking Bad), the affecting boxing flick Kairos, and the doco Finke: There & Back, while Aussie director Cameron Nugent makes his mark in the US with A Boy Called Sailboat. Also out this week: the creepy, kooky and altogether ooky Addams Family, the doco Where's My Roy Cohn?, and the comedy Dying To Survive. And don't forget that The Japanese Film Festival is continuing its journey around the nation right now! See you at the movies!

THE LATEST
Breaking Bad's R.J Mitte Is Standing Up For Sunny
Young actor R.J Mitte - best known for playing Bryan Cranston's son on the landmark TV series Breaking Bad - heads Down Under for the lead role in the local comedy drama Standing Up For Sunny. 

Paul Barakat: Kairos

Aussie director Paul Barakat delivers big time with Kairos, the moving story of a young man born with Down Syndrome who dreams of being a boxer.
Cameron Nugent: Boy Called Sailboat
Aussie director Cameron Nugent pulls off a major coup by tapping Oscar winner J.K Simmons to star in his US drama A Boy Called Sailboat. 

The 2019 AACTA Awards

The 2019 AACTA Awards happened last night, with The Nightingale the biggest winner, along with Sam Neill, who picked up The Longford Lyell Award.
Rosie Lourde: Romance On The Menu
Australian actress turned director Rosie Lourde has commenced shooting in Brisbane on her filled-with-food rom-com Romance On The Menu. 

Three Identical Strangers

Producer Becky Read tells all about the acclaimed Three Identical Strangers, one of the most talked about documentaries of recent times.
The Films Of Errol Flynn: Part 5
In this fifth part of an ongoing series, we look at the key works of one of Australia's first - and most divisive - international movie stars.
