• Stocks mixed as June begins, oil nears $68 level

    The Dow Jones Industrial Average squeaked out a modest gain with Boeing rallying over 3% as the month of June kicked off. This as the Nasdaq Composite and S&P 500 ended little changed. In stock news, AMC jumped 22% after the company announced a $230 million capital raise, investors also eyed a steady climb in oil prices ahead of OPEC’s meeting.
