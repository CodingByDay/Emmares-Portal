It's been a busy week! We visited the annual Toy Fair 2020 in New York City and spent time with tons of upcoming playthings. For work of course. Some trends we noticed? A whole lot of toys are being made with eco-friendly materials in mind, like the products from Green Toys, which are made from 100 percent recycled materials. But a toy fair in 2020 wouldn't be complete without Baby Yoda. Yes, you'll soon be able to have your own impossibly cute plush version.

The week has also been quite somber, with news of the first community-spread Covid-19 case and the Dow Jones plummeted even further. WIRED Senior writer, Arielle Pardes, also took a look at how Silicon Valley ruined work culture everywhere.

To end on a happier note, we've added several new reviews and updated roundups this week. First, you can check out our Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra review, as well as our review on Outdoor Research's MicroGravity jacket. We also tested new pet cameras, and have a first look at LG's new V60 ThinQ smartphone.
