If you’ve ever wondered whether it’s a good idea to buy an IBM computer, reverse engineer it, and use the BIOS to build a better computer for your company to sell, I can tell you it’s probably not a good idea. You’ll probably get in trouble. I know the guys in Halt and Catch Fire sure did because I’ve been watching this show recently.

It follows three characters who do just that in an effort to build a personal computer that works twice as fast and is half the size of IBM’s. I love the show for its subject material and killer soundtrack, but also because of the fantastic characters and writing. Whether you’re a computer geek or not, you’ll should check out Halt and Catch Fire.
