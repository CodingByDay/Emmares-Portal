Understanding the immune system has never been so important. This new online course serves as a primer on the biology of the immune system, how it works and what you can do to keep it healthy.

Under their guidance, and with plenty of additional course material, you will gain a deeper understanding of how the immune system works. You will learn how it changes as we age, and how it sometimes mistakenly responds to targets that pose no threat to our health, such as dust and pollen. You will also hear the latest thinking on autoimmune conditions, characterised by an immune system that begins to attack an individual’s healthy tissue instead of harmful pathogens.
