New Skift Research — Sustainability in Travel 2021: Environmental Performance of the Six Largest Airlines

Aviation contributes about 2% of global greenhouse gas emissions, and while far from the most polluting industry, is receiving more and more public scrutiny.

In Skift Research’s newly released “Sustainability in Travel 2021: Environmental Performance of the Six Largest Airlines”, we analyze and compare current emission reduction performance of the six largest airline companies: Air France-KLM, American Airlines, Delta Airlines, International Airlines Group, Lufthansa, and United Airlines. 

What You'll Learn From This Report:

  • The airline company leaders and laggards in emission reduction.
  • How the aviation industry tracks and reports its emissions, and how this can be improved.
  • The targets set to reduce emissions by airline bodies and individual airlines.
  • How close the largest airlines are to achieving short-, medium-, and long-term goals. 
  • The shortcomings of current metrics and initiatives to meet medium- and long-term goals.
