  MovieMaker Production Services


PITCH your film project IN PERSON for a chance to be selected for the MovieMaker Production Services program.

MovieMaker magazine's Editor-in-Chief Tim Rhys is set to head up the MMPS Pitchfest at the Local Sightings Film Festival in Seattle, WA on Sunday September 22nd, 2019 at 2pm.

The MMPS program is a unique industry initiative that provides a bountiful budget boost by doubling a film producer's spending power for each dollar invested.

If you'll be starting principal photography on a film project in the next 36 months and have at least $25,000 in cash already raised for a documentary or narrative feature, or $10,000 for a short film, you may be eligible to pitch your project to us face-to-face in Seattle.

For more eligibility requirements see here:
https://filmfreeway.com/MovieMakerProductionServices
Contact N.L. Brooks, MM's Festivals & Partnerships Liaison at nlbrooks@moviemaker.com with questions or to discuss scheduling an in-person pitch.

                Film San Antonio


Recognized as a top film-friendly city, San Antonio is the home of film incentives up to 30 percent, no film permit fees, film-friendly locations and accommodations, experienced cast and crew, and a ready-to-help Film Commission. The Film Commission helps the production process by managing film permits, connecting productions with local cast and crew, and providing liaison services between the production, City departments, businesses and residents. As the seventh largest city in Texas with more than 300 years of history, San Antonio is a home away from home for productions, both on and offset. Discover all San Antonio has to offer at FilmSanAntonio.com.

            OnSET Film School


Film Schools often charge staggering fees. The lack of real world experience students actually receive doesn't add up. 

OnSET FILM SCHOOL N.Y.C. is hands on in the development and physical production process with a focus on the business of entertainment. All in a real-time, real-world production environment, at a fraction of the price. End of year one, we produce a narrative feature film that you will work on from script to screen, learning every facet of the process. All fees are an investment in that film. You will recoup your tuition (+15%) upon distribution as a producer. 

Our partners at Emmy award-winning Pipeline Entertainment will also work diligently to place you in the marketplace upon graduation.
