June 21, 2021

Dear Reader,

COVAX, the effort to distribute vaccines to the rest of the world, is falling short of its goal of distributing two billion doses by the end of 2021, in part because of a shortage of available doses. In the U.S., states and companies have been offering cash lotteries, free beer and other incentives to encourage COVID vaccination. And the Supreme Court upheld the Affordable Care Act for the third time.

Tanya Lewis, Senior Editor, Health & Medicine
