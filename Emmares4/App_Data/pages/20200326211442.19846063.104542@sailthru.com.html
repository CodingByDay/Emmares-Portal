This week, people across the country settled into a new, socially distanced norm. As the pandemic intensifies, so too have physical and social isolation. At Gadget Lab, we've been thinking about the ways technology can keep us connected, sane, and entertained as we stay home and #flattenthecurve.

Being confined poses a lot of challenges—especially when you add out-of-school children to the mix. If you're hoping to use the time home to check some items off your list, here are some tips for finally tackling the things you've been putting off. Perhaps even more important to your productivity, here are some ways to keep those little ones entertained so you can actually take a minute to do those things. (No judgement if you'd prefer to put on some noise-canceling headphones every once in a while instead.)

If staying connected with people beyond the walls of the house is your objective, we've got you covered there too. We've compiled our favorite apps and services for keeping in touch, along with a handy guide on how to up your Zoom game for the many video calls to come.

Lastly, we spoke with a pediatric infectious disease expert for some important advice on how to continue caring for your kids if you do come down with Covid-19. We hope you and your loved ones stay happy and healthy, and our stories, reviews, and recommendations will continue providing support on those fronts. These are not normal times, but it's our hope that with technology, we can simulate a sense of normalcy as we get through this together.
