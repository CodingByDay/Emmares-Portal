The pandemic forced businesses to accelerate digital transformation by a decade in a matter of months. Despite initial hesitation to fully embrace virtual work environments, the workforce has made it clear that hybrid work is here to stay.

 

So what does hybrid work mean for the offices of today and tomorrow, and how can 5G and integrated technologies empower workers from anywhere? Find out on August 25 in a fireside chat discussion with networks and display leaders from Samsung, where you’ll discover how to: 

  • Equip your workforce with the latest technology
  • Reimagine how and where work gets done
  • Build near- and long-term digital strategies
