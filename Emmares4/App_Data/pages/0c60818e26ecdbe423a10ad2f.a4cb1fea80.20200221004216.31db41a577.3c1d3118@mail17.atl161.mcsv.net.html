One More Thing

Source: Amy Lombard for NBC News

Chess is emerging as an e-sport, offering pros and fans alike a new way to make a living via livestreaming. 

 

The relatively small market has grown quickly: time spent watching chess has risen by more than 500% since 2016, according to Twitch. 

 

While real-world chess matches can last hours and often end in a draw, livestreamed matches may last less than 10 minutes and can bring levity to the oft-serious game.
