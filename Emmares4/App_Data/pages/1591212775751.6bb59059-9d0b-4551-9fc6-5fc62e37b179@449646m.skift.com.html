What She Knows

Sigríður Dögg heads Visit Iceland, the official destination marketing office for Iceland. She leads a platform for cooperation with domestic and foreign tour operators and other stakeholders in Icelandic tourism, as well as integrated marketing initiatives to consumers.

What She'll Share

As Iceland opens up its doors to tourists on June 15, how will visitors be made to feel safe and welcomed? Iceland may be one of the first countries to come up with an organized plan to reopen tourism, post-crisis. With no cap on visitor numbers planned, how will Iceland manage them upon arrival -- and will they come? Sigríður Dögg will share their reopening progress and their marketing efforts around safety, as well as the initial reaction from travelers.
