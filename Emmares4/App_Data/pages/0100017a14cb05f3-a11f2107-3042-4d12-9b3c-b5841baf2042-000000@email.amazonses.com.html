Well, this isn’t how I expected my day to go. I abandoned all my plans in order to try out Windows 11. Yeah, you read that right. Windows 11, as in an unannounced upcoming Windows OS that will soon replace Windows 10. And you know the old saying, “the more things change, the more they stay the same?” That pretty much applies here.

When you first fire up Windows 11, it looks really different. Some of the changes are good (death to Live Tiles!). Some not so good (I like having the Taskbar on the side of my screen, please give me that option back). But the deeper you dig, the more it feels like Windows 10 with a shiny new skin. We have all the details and even the new Windows Startup noise.
