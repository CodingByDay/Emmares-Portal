Dear Reader,

We often think of human modification of landscapes and waterways as a modern behavior, but new evidence suggests that early modern humans altered a forest on the northern shores of Lake Malawi in Africa more than 80 million years ago. Read Katarina Zimmer's coverage below to learn more about the evidence as well as questions surrounding whether the fires were deliberate or not.
