Todd Gurley was cut by the Rams this afternoon ahead of a deadline that would have guaranteed him an additional $10.5 million. With Gurley hitting the free agency market, we've picked the three best spots for him to sign. While there isn't a team that would make him a top 10 RB, Gurley could provide dividends as an RB2 in 2020 if the fit is right.

The Rams ran the ball over 400 times in 2019, a workload that will likely be split between Darrell Henderson and Malcolm Brown if the Rams don't add another RB. Henderson was a highly-touted prospect out of Memphis, but Brown earned the trust of McVay last season, making this a tough split to decipher right now.

And don't think news can break without an emergency podcast from the FFT crew! We take 15 minutes to break down the Gurley release, debating the ideal landing spot, workload split of the Rams RBs, and why Tyler Higbee might be today's biggest winner.
