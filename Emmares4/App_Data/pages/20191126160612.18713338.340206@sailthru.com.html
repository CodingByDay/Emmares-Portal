Instant Pot® Creamy Herb Pork

Cozy up this Thanksgiving and holiday season with this insanely creamy Instant Pot® pork recipe!

Mouthwateringly tender and seasoned to perfection, this creamy pork shoulder is *so* juicy and brimming full of flavor that we're sure the gang coming over for the holidays will keep coming back for seconds!

 

After generously seasoning your pork shoulder, sear the meat in your Instant Pot® on all sides until brown. Once you remove the meat from the pot and set it aside, deglaze the pot with some white wine and toss the meat back in alongside a handful of Private Selection Petite Red Potatoes, Kroger Baby Carrots, chicken broth, and thyme. After cooking under high pressure for fifty minutes, you're just about done!
