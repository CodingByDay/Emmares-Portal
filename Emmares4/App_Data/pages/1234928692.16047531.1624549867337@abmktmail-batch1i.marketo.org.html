The World's Largest Purpose-Driven Startup Competition

TechCrunch just announced the agenda for the Extreme Tech Challenge Global Finals on July 22 featuring representatives from the United Nations, NXP, Urban US, and many more! The Global Finals is the world's largest transformative-tech startup competition powering a more sustainable, equitable, inclusive, and healthy world.

 

The event features a global startup pitch competition where 10 startups from around the world will be competing for the top spot and join an alumni network that's raised a cumulative $1.8B from leading VCs.

 

In addition to the startup competition, we're bringing in a range of founders, VCs, and corporate leaders who are going to be talking about tech that makes the world a better place. Following the show, we will have a networking session for all attendees to meet and mingle.

 

Check out the agenda and speaker highlights below. Sign up today for your free registration and join us on July 22.
