Modern gadgets are exceedingly easy to set up. Technology has advanced to the point where most tech products can connect to your phone or the internet in a few minutes with just a couple of taps in an app.

But that's actually not where the setup process ends. Before you dive in and start using whatever lovely gadget you unwrapped this week, it's important to understand how it works, what data it collects, and how to disable the things you don't want it to do. Or maybe there are some crucial settings that improve the experience—the ones the manufacturer usually doesn't tell people about.

That's why we're here. Our master guide to setting up all your stuff outlines all of the helpful advice our team of gadget experts has collected over the years. We share this hard-won knowledge about iPhones, Alexa speakers, Nest thermostats, 4K televisions, and more so that you can start getting the most out of the new gadgets in your life. And if you're bent on returning that gift, we have a guide for that too.
