New Skift Research — Airbnb and the Short-Term Rental Market 2020

Airbnb is probably the most successful and influential travel company of the last decade. Now its latest disclosures let us dig into the company and understand its business in a way never before possible.

Skift Research’s latest report on Airbnb and the Short-Term Rental Market 2020 analyzes Airbnb’s market position by breaking down the key areas of Airbnb’s business.

What You’ll Learn From This Report:

  • Proprietary estimates of Airbnb’s growth rates and segment reporting, including Experiences and China
  • Detailed data on Airbnb’s marketing efforts and its brand position to attract, retain, and grow customers
  • An analysis of the types of listings and property managers on Airbnb, including a look at how much inventory is exclusive to the platform
  • A deep dive into Airbnb’s COVID-19 response
  • The size of the short-term rental market in 2020 and Skift Research’s updated ecosystem overview
