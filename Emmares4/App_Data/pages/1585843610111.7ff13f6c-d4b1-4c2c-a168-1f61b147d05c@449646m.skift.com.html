New Skift Research — The Impact of COVID-19 on the Hotel Industry

Hit hard by the rapid spread of COVID-19, the hotel industry is on its knees, while at the same time stepping up to offer its services and help defeat this pandemic. The industry is truly at the epicenter of the coronavirus crisis.

There are a lot of sources providing insights about the impact of the coronavirus on the hotel industry, but it is hard to keep track when you’re head down taking things day by day. This report attempts to provide some clarity by offering a single narrative through the million voices and disparate snippets of data that are available. We follow the timeline of the COVID-19 virus spread as a guide through these discussions, focusing on China, Italy, Spain, and the U.S. to see what has happened so far, and what is to come. 

Recovery will arrive, but when and in what shape is wholly uncertain. Understanding the current situation, and what a recovery might look like, will help the industry to prepare for when that day comes.

What You’ll Learn From This Report

  • How the coronavirus has spread around the world, and how this has impacted hotel performance.

  • Key performance indicators of hotels in hard hit countries including China, Italy, Spain, and the U.S.

  • A discussion on what the road ahead looks like for the hotel industry.

  • The impact this crisis is having on the hotel tech vendor landscape.
