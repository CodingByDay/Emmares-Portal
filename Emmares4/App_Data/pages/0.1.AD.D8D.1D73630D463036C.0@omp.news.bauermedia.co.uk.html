CAR Magazine interviews Lotus' Managing Director Matt Windle, as the seeds of a significant investment from parent company Geely begin to bear fruit. Ahead of him lies revitalising a once-abandoned factory expansion and forging the foundations for ambitious growth plans.

Lotus Evija: electric hypercar
The Lotus Type 130 - or Evija - showcases the benefits of an electric powertrain in a near-2000bhp hypercar. Limited to 130 examples, at £2.4 million each, this is not the future of Lotus per se - but it does demonstrate what Lotus is capable of and, perhaps, a future direction for the marque.
