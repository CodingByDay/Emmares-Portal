Google’s new Drive app replaces Backup and Sync with Drive File Stream

Weirdly the new app still doesn’t support files or folders that are shared with you. It only shows you shared business drives if your Google Workspace account supports that.

Also weirdly on Mac, at least, the app works by mounting a localhost proxy. If you mistakenly close that proxy it breaks until you restart.

Bottom line: Google Drive for desktop is still hot garbage compared to Dropbox.

Apple AirPod battery life problem shows need for right-to-repair laws

AirPod batteries are almost impossible to replace because they are extremely tiny devices. They are constantly used as an example for right to repair, but it makes no sense.

What’s the remedy? Force companies like Apple to only sell huge devices that can be easily repaired? If customers really cared, they wouldn’t buy them in the first place.
