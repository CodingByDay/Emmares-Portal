Enterprise steps up for college kids forced to leave campus amid the COVID-19 pandemic.
Want to .
All across the U.S. and Canada, college kids are being evacuated from campuses amid the coronavirus pandemic. To help students get home safely and ease the burden on families during this time, Enterprise Rent-A-Car is offering College Student Travel Assistance, a program that reduces the minimum age and waives young renter fees for rentals through May 31, 2020. This is a big deal, since young renter fees can be $30 per day and, normally, you have to be at least 21 years old to rent with Enterprise.

PRO TIP: AutoSlash can recommend multiple ways for college kids to easily avoid young renter fees through memberships like AAA and USAA. AAA members aged 20 to 24 can take advantage of a Young Driver Fee waiver at Hertz. For USAA members, waivers at Avis, Budget and Hertz start at age 18, while the fee waiver at Enterprise starts at the age of 21.
