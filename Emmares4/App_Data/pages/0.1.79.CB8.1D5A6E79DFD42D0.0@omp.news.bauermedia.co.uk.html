Exclusive Episode IX: The Rise Of Skywalker Issue of Empire

42 years after it all began, the saga ends. Since 1977, Star Wars’ central Skywalker Saga has delivered an epic story of good and evil, light side and dark side, rebels and overlords caught in an intergalactic conflict. And now, with Episode IX: The Rise Of Skywalker, the multi-generational family story comes to a close. 

To celebrate, Empire presents its biggest ever Star Wars issue, with three exclusive covers to collect. Inside the mag you’ll find brand new images and insights from the final chapter, and all-new interviews with Sequel Trilogy stars Daisy Ridley, Adam Driver, John Boyega, Oscar Isaac, and Kelly Marie Tran, the legendary Anthony Daniels and Billy Dee Williams, franchise newcomers Naomi Ackie and Keri Russell, and writer-director J.J. Abrams and co-writer Chris Terrio. Not only that, but the issue also comes with a bonus Skywalker Saga magazine, looking back across all eight films so far with fresh interviews, features, archival material, and more – plus a Dark Rey art card, illustrated exclusively for Empire by Rory Kurtz. 

It’s everything you need to get ready for the arrival of Episode IX. May the Force be with you.
