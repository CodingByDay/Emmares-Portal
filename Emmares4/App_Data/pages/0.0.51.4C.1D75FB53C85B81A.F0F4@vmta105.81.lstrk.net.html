A Nicaraguan puro, the Aging Room Quattro Nicaragua is a stunning smoke with deep, rich flavors and outstanding construction. It was blended by Rafael Nodal and AJ Fernandez, using tobaccos grown on AJ's farms throughout Nicaragua.
