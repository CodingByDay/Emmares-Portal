News and events from COST Actions 

Special feature | 11 May | Sustainable European cities and digitization
The Council for European Studies has released a special feature on 'Sustainable European cities and digitization' in the EuropeNow journal, partly thanks to the work of two COST Actions: Dynamics of placemaking and digitization in Europe’s cities, and Research network of including geothermal technologies into decarbonised heating and cooling grids. This month's issue offers insights into developing the sustainability of European cities through a number of case studies of recent social and technological practices, while also foregrounding the role of the digital in modernization.
Read more...

Paper | 26 May | Global commitments to conserving and monitoring genetic diversity are now necessary and feasible
A new paper in the journal BioScience signed by 28 scientists representing 16 countries committed to tackle the hidden biodiversity crisis. Cristiano Vernesi, chair of the COST Action G-BiKE, contributed to the study.
Read the full paper...

Event | 1 June | Families in positive + European Family Support Network: projects to promote the positive parenting approach in child and family services
The Spanish Federation of Municipalities and Provinces and the Ministry of Social Rights and 2030 Agenda, in collaboration with the European Council of Municipalities and Regions and the COST Action European Family Support Network (EuroFamNet), are organising this open webinar to share the progress of the projects Families in positive and EuroFamNet with the European local associations and other European stakeholders (NGOs, professional associations, etc.).
Read more...

Event | 2 June | Computer-assisted legal translation in practice
This conference is co-organised by the WG2 of the COST Action Language in the human-machine era. Speakers will present about the widespread use of computer-assisted translation (CAT) tools. Free online registration is open.
Read more...

Event | 2 June | COST Online Info Day - Cyprus
The aim of this webinar is to provide information to Cyprus-based researchers and innovators about the networking opportunities provided by COST on how to get involved in a COST Action and how to prepare a new proposal. Free online registration is open.
Read more...

Event | 4 June | EUSEA-COST Hot Pot: Public engagement beyond COVID – Where do we go from here?
The aim of this interactive Hot Pot, jointly organised by the European Science Engagement Association and the COST Cross-Cutting Activity on Science Communication, is to explore lessons learned, share reflections and co-create practical recommendations for science communication and public engagement strategies derived from the global pandemic. Free online registration is open.
Read more...

Event | 7-9 June | Toposuper2021 – Emergent topological superconductivity
This online conference organised by the COST Action Nanoscale coherent hybrid devices for superconducting quantum technologies focuses on the topological superconductivity in low-dimensional correlated systems. Free online registration is open.
Read more...

Event | 18 June | Family Support & COVID. Learning and responding from a European perspective
The aim of this open webinar by the COST Action European Family Support Network is to encourage mutual knowledge exchange in Family Support research and practice across Europe during the COVID-19 situation, and to encourage collaboration and joint initiatives that contribute to the field of Family Support and the Network. Free online registration is open.

Call for applications | deadlines 31 May, 15 July, 1 August | Rural NEET Youth Network’s Spring 2021 Calls
The COST Action Rural NEET youth network: modeling the risks underlying rural NEETs social exclusion, is calling for applications to their Spring 2021 Calls. Selected applicants will be able to publish papers, attend online and face-to-face conferences, visit some institution for a short research, organice events to disseminate the Action, and more!

The COST Action Towards an international network for evidence-based research in clinical health research is calling for abstracts for their conference 'The place of evidence-based research in the evidence ecosystem', taking place online on 27-28 September 2021.

Call for applications | deadline 14 June | Summer School on Workplace Management 2021

Call for applications | deadline 15 June | Second Underground4Value Training School

Call for applications | deadline 30 June | STRATAGEM's Training School: Targeting MDR Tumours: from natural product chemistry to nanocarrier-based formulation
