On December 9-10 we're bringing together some of the top CEOs, business leaders, thinkers and innovators across the travel industry for the second annual Skift Short-Term Rental & Outdoor Summit.

 

With the event taking place fully online, we’re excited to see the summit continue to evolve. Not only will speakers explore the topic of short-term rentals in new ways this year, but we've expanded our focus to include outdoor recreation, given that traveler interest in wide-open spaces has boomed in an era of social distancing.

 

Here's what we’ll discuss:

  • Has the pandemic change the way people book accommodations for good? 
  • What are the on-demand marketplaces for outdoor travel that have boomed out of this crisis and will they continue to grow?
  • How will the hotel and short-term rental sectors converge out of this crisis?
  • What are the emerging technologies that will impact the traveler experience, streamline operations, and drive growth? 
  • How will governments consider regulation as the industry both professionalizes and grows?
