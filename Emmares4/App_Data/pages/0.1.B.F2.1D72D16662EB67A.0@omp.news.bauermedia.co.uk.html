WELCOME TO EMPIRE
Welcome to the Empire mailing list, you’re now all signed up to receive regular emails from the world’s biggest film magazine. But why stop there? Empire has so much more to offer than the occasional missive in your inbox. For starters, we have all the latest news and reviews over at empireonline.com. Plus you can get near instant updates by following us on Twitter, Facebook and Instagram @empiremagazine. Want to make sure you have the latest copy of Empire magazine? The Empire film Podcast
 
Our award-winning flagship podcast first launched in 2012 and has evolved from four idiots talking about films in a cupboard to four idiots talking about films in a studio. With theme music. The Empire Film Podcast brings you all the latest movie news and reviews, all accompanied by the very best in irreverent, film-related chat and boasting regular interviews with some of the biggest names in Hollywood.

The Spoiler Special Podcast
 
If you’re not content with surface-level detail and want to dive deeper then The Empire Spoiler Special Podcast is for you. This subscriber-only podcast takes you inside movies and TV shows like never before. Tackling all the major plot twists and story points, these hugely in-depth shows aim to mine every last secret from the latest releases (as well as the occasional classic), frequently inviting filmmakers to join us for an all-access breakdown of the biggest films and shows.

The Pilot TV Podcast
 
Empire’s TV-themed sister podcast is your (spoiler free) guide to the essential new shows dropping each week across terrestrial, satellite, streaming and beyond. Bringing you the latest news and reviews, the occasional interview, and some choice recommendations from years past, Pilot TV is here to cut through the rubbish and make sure every minute you spend in front of the box is a minute worth spending.
