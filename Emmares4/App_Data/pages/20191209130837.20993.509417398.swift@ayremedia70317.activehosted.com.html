Trouble in paradise
 

The Story

Leisure & Resorts World Corporation (LRWC), the supposed partner of Galaxy Entertainment in building a casino in the world-famous Boracay Island in the Philippines, has backed out. In a statement, LRWC President Eusebio Tanco said that the company would not pursue the $550 million integrated resort project. "We are not looking at casino. But I’m keeping that piece of land. I’m keeping that piece of land and will just hold on to it. I will just land bank it," Tanco said. Despite the news, it appears that Galaxy Entertainment Group Ltd will continue to try to move ahead with the project. PAGCOR CEO Alfredo C. Lim explained that the "environmentally-friendly approach" that Galaxy proposed still makes the casino a viable project.
