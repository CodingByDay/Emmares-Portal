Small Steps to 401(k) Greatness
Fun fact: Today is National 401(k) Day!

If you don't already have a 401(k)—and your employer offers the option—celebrate by opening one today. If your job doesn't have a 401(k) plan, there are other retirement accounts you can contribute to in the meantime, such as a Roth or Traditional IRA or, if you're self-employed, a SEP IRA.

Already have a 401(k) or another retirement account set up? Challenge yourself to increase your contribution by at least 1% over the next couple of months. It's a small step, but small steps will help you reach your long-term savings goals.
