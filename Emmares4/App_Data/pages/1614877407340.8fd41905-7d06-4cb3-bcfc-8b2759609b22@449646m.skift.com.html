New Skift Research — Venture Investment Trends in Travel 2021

Skift Research’s annual Venture Investment Trends in Travel report is here.

Venture capitalists invested $4.9B of new capital into the travel industry in 2020, a whopping 55% decline from 2019.

However, the U.S. and Canada saw continued growth in investment and many travel "unicorns" with market value at over $1 billon continued to raise new funds and grow their valuations even in the teeth of the crisis. 

What You'll Learn From This Report:

  • The size of the travel startup financing market, 2011–2020
  • Key 2020 funding trends In travel, including top themes and regions
  • Top 2020 investors in the travel space, by number of deals, deal sizes and region
  • Major 2020 funding deals by region, size, company, and sector
  • Analysis of exit opportunities, including top acquirers of travel startups and special purpose acquisition corporations (SPACs)
