Chagrin Documentary Film Festival
 
The Chagrin Documentary Film Festival is dedicated to educating audiences and empowering talented filmmakers to tell their stories. The Festival is a five day celebration of the art of documentary film and draws audiences from all over Northeast Ohio, the US and the world to experience documentary films and the compelling art and culture they highlight. "The entire team at CDFF put on one of the best documentary film festivals in America. They treat the filmmakers like royalty and the entire town gets behind the festival making it a truly unforgettable experience." - 2019 Filmmaker 
            Austin Film Commission


Austin is a leading destination for film, television and commercial productions. The State of Texas offers productions up to a 22.5% rebate and Austin's talented crew base give you more production value per-dollar spent. In addition, local government supports filmmakers, providing free right-of-way permitting. Austin is surrounded by Americana small towns, hill country, waterways and ranches - all within 30 minutes of a diverse, modern downtown. The infrastructure is in place for filmmaking, including over a dozen of studio spaces and equipment suppliers. With all of this and over 300 days of sunshine a year - Austin is the perfect location for your next production. AustinFilmCommission.com 
San Luis Obispo International Film Festival


The 26th San Luis Obispo International Film Festival (SLO Film Fest) is happening from March 17th to 22nd, 2020 in downtown San Luis Obispo and surrounding towns. Located half-way between LA and SF, San Luis Obispo's laid-back vibe and serene natural beauty is the ideal setting for this highly regarded festival that filmmakers rave about. Known for its thoughtful programming, great networking opportunities and its uber-friendly hospitality and community involvement, filmmakers are the stars! Highlights include parties every night, a Filmmaker Lounge with complimentary wine and beer, enthusiast audiences and a concierge program. At the SLO Film Fest, "Movies Matter!" Learn More

                Film San Antonio


Recognized as a top film-friendly city, San Antonio is the home of film incentives up to 30 percent, no film permit fees, film-friendly locations and accommodations, experienced cast and crew, and a ready-to-help Film Commission. The Film Commission helps the production process by managing film permits, connecting productions with local cast and crew, and providing liaison services between the production, City departments, businesses and residents. As the seventh largest city in Texas with more than 300 years of history, San Antonio is a home away from home for productions, both on and offset. Discover all San Antonio has to offer at FilmSanAntonio.com.
