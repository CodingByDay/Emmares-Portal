THE LATEST
Timothy Spall Gets Painterly With Mrs. Lowry & Son
After expertly essaying a famous painter in Mike Leigh's Mr. Turner, British master actor Timothy Spall digs deep with another hypnotic portrayal of an artist in Mrs. Lowry & Son, where he breathes haunting life into divisive figure L.S Lowry.  

Selina Miles: Martha: A Picture Story

Australian filmmaker Selina Miles documents the fascinating life of a seminal New York outsider artist with her new film Martha: A Picture Story. 
Helen Dallimore: Two Heads Creek
Aussie actress Helen Dallimore goes mind blowingly and hilariously over the top as a smalltown nightmare in the horror comedy Two Heads Creek. 

Alicia Brown From Film Victoria

Film Victoria Production Executive Alicia Brown pulls back the curtain on the often fraught state of play for current and future film production in Australia.
Damson Idris: Farming
The British star of the acclaimed television series Snowfall returns home to take the lead in Adewale Akinnuoye-Agbaje’s Farming.

Celine Sciamma On Art And Passion

The against-the-grain French director (Girlhood, Water Lillies, Tomboys) delivers her first period film with the award winning Portrait Of A Lady On Fire.
The Films Of Errol Flynn: Part 4
In this fourth part of an ongoing series, we look at the key works of one of Australia's first - and most divisive - international movie stars.
