Keep up with the rapidly expanding short-term rental ecosystem
 
Over the last 10 years the average growth per year in the short-term rental sector is over 12%. How are you adapting to this rapidly expanding ecosystem? Join us this December 5th in NYC for Skift Short-Term Rental Summit.
 

The focus of this intense one-day summit will include discussing key players, mapping the landscape and identifying growth potential, highlighting emerging technologies, sharing new research and insights on the latest government regulations, and discussing business and landscape challenges.
 
 

TOPICS INCLUDE:

  • Where’s the money in an ever-expanding ecosystem?
  • Professionalization of the sector and the importance of branding
  • Changing consumer demands
  • The role of platforms to achieve sustainable growth
  • Is the independent host worth chasing?
  • and more!
