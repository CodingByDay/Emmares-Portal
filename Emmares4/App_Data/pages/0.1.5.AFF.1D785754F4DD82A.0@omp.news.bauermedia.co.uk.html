When worlds collide: M5 CS faces RS e-Tron GT
In a week where the efforts of the many to be green are outweighed by the achievements of one wanting to see space, the question of CO2 vs your personal gratification is pretty relevant. In the grand scheme of things, there are so few BMW M5s out there that enjoying the fossil-fuelled brute can be fairly guilt-free - but why waste the thought when Audi wants to tempt you with the e-Tron GT? Is the roaring dinosaur of ICE better off extinct?

Now's a great time if you want to sit on the fence though - plug-in hybrids are everywhere, from Hyundai to Bentley (scroll down for examples), and if you want to relive the days of classic high-performance repmobiles before BMW and Mercedes took over the company car park - Peugeot and Volvo have just what you ordered.

Plus, we look at the next off-road racers from Prodrive and Bowler, get into the 2012 Mercedes-AMG C 63 Black Series, discover one ICE goddess that has found a spark of life after petrol, and there's a new small SUV from VW.

Enjoy, and have a great weekend!
