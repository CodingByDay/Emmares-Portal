We’re looking forward to the Skift Online Travel and Distribution Summit taking place on February 17. Here are the questions we'll address.

Join us on February 17 for Skift's Online Travel and Distribution Summit focusing on the highly-competitive and ever-evolving online travel and distribution ecosystem.

 

Taking place online from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. EST, this tactical and in-depth online summit will dive into the emerging products, consumer shifts, and transformations happening in online travel and distribution through CEO and senior leader interviews, panels, and roundtables.

 

Register now for this summit as we address the big questions facing this sector:

  • How will online travel agencies bounce back?
  • Who will IPO or be acquired in 2021?
  • What is the role of metasearch and does it still have the clout it once did?
  • Who are the upstarts that might dethrone the giants?
  • How can we learn from China’s growth and innovations in distribution?
  • What are the marketing challenges facing online travel agencies and metasearch engines coming out of the pandemic?
  • Has the pandemic led to a deeper de-commoditization of supply?
  • Does the pandemic bring us back towards distribution, or have suppliers mastered direct?
  • And more.

Can’t attend live? Recordings and presentations will be available on-demand for registered attendees after the Forum.
