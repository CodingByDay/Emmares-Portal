New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: May 2021 Highlights

The Skift Recovery Index continued its upward journey in May 2021, with the global average score hitting the 60% mark, indicating that the overall travel performance of May 2021 stands at 60% of the pre-pandemic performance in May 2019. 

In Skift Research's latest Skift Recovery Index report, we highlight recovery scores by country, region and travel sector in May.  In addition, we present our analysis of how vaccination rates, new case counts, and local mobility correlate with travel recovery and discuss the possibilities of using these types of data to predict travel performance in the coming weeks and months.
