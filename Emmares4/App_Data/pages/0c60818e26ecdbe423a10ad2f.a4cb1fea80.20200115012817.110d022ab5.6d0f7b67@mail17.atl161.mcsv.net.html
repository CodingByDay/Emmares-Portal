Looking for love in all the right places

Hi there,

 

What good is commercialized space travel if you have to fly solo?

 

Yusaku Maezawa, a Japanese billionaire and first prospective rider on SpaceX’s deep-space rocket, has launched a campaign to find a significant other to join him on a trip around the moon.

 

The contest is open to women who are older than 20, “want to enjoy life to the fullest,” and “wish for world peace.”

 

Maezawa’s search for lunar love will naturally be turned into a reality show called “Full Moon Lovers.”

 

What a time to be alive.

Get Plaid, get paid
 

Yesterday, Visa announced that it’s buying fintech startup Plaid for a cool $5.3B. Plaid is the engine that powers popular consumer apps such as Robinhood, Venmo, Coinbase, and Betterment, among others.

 

The CEO and co-founder of Plaid will be speaking at the Future of Fintech (June 14-16, SF).

 

Also on the lineup are speakers from Banco Santander, Citigroup, MoneyLion, JPMorgan Chase, Wealthfront, TransferWise, Dave.com, and more.
