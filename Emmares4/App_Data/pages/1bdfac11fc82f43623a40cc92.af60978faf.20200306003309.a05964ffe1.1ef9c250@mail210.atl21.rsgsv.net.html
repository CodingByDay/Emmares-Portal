USE ME: SYDNEY SCREENING AND Q&A MARCH 10

"Blurring the line between fantasy and reality in ways both subtle and overt, it keeps you guessing right up until the end." (FilmInk) 

 
Acclaimed documentarian Julian Shaw (Cup Of Dreams, Darling: The Pieter-Dirk Uys Story) wades into the weird and dangerous world of fetishism with Use Me, a canny mix of documentary and fictional filmmaking. The film revolves around Ceara Lynch, a real life online humiliatrix who berates her adoring clients for money, and also gets them off by running up their credit cards, putting them on hold and ignoring them, and wreaking havoc on their financial lives. In a powerful display of domination, the impossibly alluring Ms. Lynch doesn’t even make actual contact with her worshipful clients. Playing out like a full throttle thriller, Use Me sees Julian Shaw head to the US to make a doco about Ceara Lynch, but he soon finds himself a partially unwilling victim in a life threatening game of cat and mouse. Exciting, disturbing, and sexy, Use Me will have you hooked from beginning to end.
