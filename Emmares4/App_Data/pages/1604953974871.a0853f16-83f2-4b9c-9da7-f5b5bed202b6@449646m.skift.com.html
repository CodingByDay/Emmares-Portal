Learn from visionary innovators and creative leaders at our first Design the Future online event taking place this Thursday.

We're just three days away from the first-ever Design The Future online event. Join us on November 12 as we bring together visionary innovators and creative leaders to discuss concepts and tactics to reimagine the traveler journey and drive the industry forward.

During the conference, attendees will hear from a host of exceptional speakers across the globe including, Aaron Walton, CEO of Walton Isaacson; Andrew Benioff, Founder & Chairman of The Independent Lodging Congress; Colin Nagy, Head of Strategy, Fred & Farid & Columnist, Skift; Jan Chipchase, Founder, Studio D; Lindsey Maxwell, Vice President, Teague, and Suchi Reddy, AIA, Founding Principal of ReddyMade Architecture & Design.
