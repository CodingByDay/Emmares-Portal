NEXT LEVEL DESIGN & TECHNOLOGY

Staying true to the G-SHOCK brand, the soon to launch smartwatch is both shock and water resistant, including an inbuilt microphone, a charging terminal and more...

 

 The case back, made of titanium, ensures excellent corrosion resistance and has a titanium carbide finish to provide enhanced abrasion resistance.

The GSW-H1000 is intricately designed for use in a wide range of activities like running, indoor workouts, road biking, and swimming, to more extreme pursuits like surfing and snowboarding.
