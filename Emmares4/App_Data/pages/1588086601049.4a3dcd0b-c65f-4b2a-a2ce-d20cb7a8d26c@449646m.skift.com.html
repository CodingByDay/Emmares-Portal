Share your thoughts in Skift’s 2020 Hospitality Recovery Survey!

The hospitality sector has been hit hard by the COVID-19 outbreak. As industry executives figure out how to navigate their business through these times, there are plenty of unanswered questions: What are their predictions about when consumers will return? How will they evolve policies and procedures for their staff? And how might the guest experience and hotel tech need to evolve to make sure travelers feel at ease?

We’re asking those in the sector to share their thoughts on what they’ll need to position themselves to succeed in the future. The results will be released in a report publishing later this summer.

Thanks again and we look forward to collaborating with you further. Please email jk@skift.com if you have any questions about the survey.
