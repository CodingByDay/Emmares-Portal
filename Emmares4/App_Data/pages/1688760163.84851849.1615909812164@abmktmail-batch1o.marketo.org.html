How to Decide Between Bootstrapping and Raising Venture

Calendly is worth more than $3 billion, and the startup spent most of its life entirely bootstrapped before taking funding from OpenView. Hear from Calendly founder and CEO Tope Awotona and OpenView partner Blake Bartlett go through the best practices for bootstrapping and when (and why) to finally take institutional capital.

What Startups Need to Know About Bug Bounties

Should all startups launch a bug bounty program to improve their security? Learn the good, the bad, and the ugly that startups will encounter when managing vulnerability disclosures and considering bug bounty programs from Katie Moussouris, Founder & CEO, Luta Security.

How to Scale a Remote-First Startup

Job van der Voort, CEO of Remote and former VP of Product at GitLab, will teach you how to onboard your first international team member; how to scale a distributed team while retaining your culture; and how to leverage your global footprint into funding, revenue, and beyond.
