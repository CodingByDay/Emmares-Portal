We're delighted to announce that our annual Skift Megatrends forecast will take place as an online event on January 13, 2021, at 11:00 am ET and on-demand.

 

Each year, Skift releases its annual team predictions on the key trends shaping the business of travel in the year to follow. This year we're doing things a little different: rather than looking at a tenuous year ahead, we embarked to envision what the broader travel world in 2025 might look like with the pandemic behind us. 

 

Here's what we'll discuss:

  • Skift Research will present an overview of its Global Travel Outlook for 2021, comparing travel segments and looking at the macro themes that will define this year ahead for the industry.
  • What will be travel's new cadence post-pandemic? Will there be a gold rush of pent-up demand or a more introspective era of travel in the decade ahead?
  • With social, economic, and environmental challenges providing a constant backdrop, what trends will spell long-term opportunity and which will prove short-lived?
  • Are we already seeing the light at the end of the tunnel? 

This carefully curated presentation will feature Skift experts presenting our trends directly to our readers and driving engaging dialogue as we work to explain these emerging shifts and their impact on sectors across travel.
