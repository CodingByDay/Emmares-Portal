...Welcome To The Week In Movies!!!
Australia’s Premier Online Movie Magazine

While we all work at flattening the curve, let's dig into some movie related content! We've got reviews of the latest titles to hit digital platforms (including Sonic The Hedgehog, Bad Boys For Life, and Bombshell), plus we talk to the makers of TV's Mystery Road, Sonic The Hedgehog and the new Netflix biopic Sergio; we've got a list of movies that were shot but never released; we take a look at two short films, Crossfire and Encircled; we track the government's lack of support for the arts community; and we ponder the proper celebrity response to lockdown. Stay safe, and (figuratively if not literally) see you at the movies!

THE LATEST
Warwick Thornton And Wayne Blair Head Down Mystery Road
The superb 2013 Australian feature film Mystery Road has been a rich source of inspiration when it comes to local storytelling, spawning a follow-up film in 2016's Goldstone and an acclaimed 2018 television series. We chat with directors Warwick Thornton and Wayne Blair about continuing the mythology with Season 2.

Jeff Fowler: Keeping Up With Sonic

The initial pre-release response to Sonic The Hedgehog was a little, ah, shaky, but director Jeff Fowler turned it around and delivered a hit. 
Arts Community Cries For Help
ScoMo & Co have just voted down all amendments moved by Labor to extend the JobKeeper wage subsidies to 110,000 artists and creatives.

Burn After Screening: Unreleased Film

Encircled Commences Production
The threat of COVID-19 might have forced the entire film industry to hit pause, but somehow the team behind Encircled have got their short up.

Opinion: Keep It Light, Celebs
