April 14, 2021

Happy Hump Day! Roku released OS 10 yesterday, and it’s packing some pretty nice features. Automatic Wi-Fi detection and instant resume are two of the big ones here, along with the ability to customize the live TV guide—because there’s nothing worse than having to scroll through 46 channels of absolute garbage to get the stuff you want. Roku TVs with OS 10 will also get automatic console config mode that will offer the best performance for game consoles, including automatic HDR, low latency, and game modes. Overall, this seems like a killer little update for Roku users.

If you’re in the market for new Roku hardware, you’re also in luck. The company announced a new rechargeable remote with voice control (micro USB charging though, boo!), along with a higher-end Streambar Pro, and the Express 4k+—the most affordable Roku with 4K. For just $40, it’s a steal.

Other highlights: 

  • Dell has a new XPS 13 with an OLED panel. Oh my. But if you’re not into that, maybe something from the new Inspiron family will work for you.
  • Logitech may have dropped the Harmony remotes, but that doesn’t mean you’re out of options when it comes to powerful universal remotes.
  • Wyze’s new lamp learns your habits to automatically adjust lighting according to your needs. We have one for testing now, so look for the full review in the coming weeks.
  • Plus: Microsoft’s new Surface Laptop 4, Mophie’s new battery that’s also an air compressor, and a lot more!
