New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: November Highlights

The latest report of the Skift Recovery Index, including data from November 1 to December 5, is now available. 

All travel verticals dipped in early November, but since then have shown some recovery towards the end of the month. By the last week of November, the global travel index score stalled at 44, indicating a 56% of performance reduction against the same week in 2019.

In this month’s highlight report, we dive deeper into the countries that have shown some of the best recoveries lately.
