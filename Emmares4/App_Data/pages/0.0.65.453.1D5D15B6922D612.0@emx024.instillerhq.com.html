Download the official Watford FC mobile app for digital tickets and more!
Watford FC is pleased to announce the launch of the new official mobile app, offering digital match tickets and exclusive content.

Due to difficult circumstances surrounding ticket sales for this weekend’s potential FA Cup fourth round tie against Manchester United (Sunday January 26), and with match ticket uploads onto Season Cards restricted to season ticket holders that purchased during the initial on-sale period between 9am – 3pm on January 16 for this fixture, we want to do all we can to ensure the number of matchday ticket collections are kept to a minimum.

So, for the first time, Hornets fans will be able to access Vicarage Road Stadium via the use of a digital ticket that can be accessed via the official Watford FC mobile app designed to reduce queues at the Ticket Office and make your matchday experience as smooth as possible.

Mobile ticketing is new to football and Watford FC will be one of the first Premier League clubs to introduce a digital ticket to supporters and whilst ongoing developments will be continuously made to improve the app-user’s experience, we are now able to offer this new service to Hornets fans.

Initially these digital tickets will be exclusive to match ticket buyers only (including all tickets sold for this weekend's potential FA Cup tie against Manchester United), however we are looking into the possibility of extending the mobile ticket service to season ticket holders for all Premier League games in future.

A FanScore account will need to be registered by simply entering your email address (if possible, please use the email address linked to your Fan ID) and creating a unique password before tickets can be accessed. All match ticket purchases linked to your Fan ID will automatically upload and will be displayed on the app within three hours of your ticket account being linked. For full ticketing instructions on the mobile app, including device specific guidelines, please click on the link below.  

The Watford FC app also includes exclusive content, offers and competitions not seen on our other channels, as well as a match centre with live audio commentary and interactive polls.

Available on iOS and Android, you can now download the Watford FC app for free and check out what's on offer - for a limited time only you will even receive a unique discount code for The Hornets Shop, in-store or online, once the app has been downloaded.
