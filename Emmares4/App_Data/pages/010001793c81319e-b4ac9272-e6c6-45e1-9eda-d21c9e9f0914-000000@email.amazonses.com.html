Happy Tuesday! If you’re still using Adobe Flash on your Windows computer, I have bad news: its time is coming to an end. I mean, let’s be honest here—we all knew it, right? Flash is a security nightmare and a garbage plugin in the first place. Sure, it was once a crucial part of the web, but that time has come and gone.

Starting next month, Microsoft will release a patch for Windows 10 that removes Flash and prevents it from being reinstalled post-update. End of an era.
