Loyalty is undergoing a reset as the travel industry navigates its way out of the
pandemic. Skift’s recent Loyalty & Subscription Summit brought together editors, analysts, and special guests to discuss how brands can capture the pent-up demand and stay relevant as travel reopens.

In this report, Skift and Salesforce partnered to recap the event’s key insights and learnings. You’ll hear from experts both in and outside of the travel industry as they discuss travel’s new frontier of subscriptions and define the new rules of loyalty.

In this Executive Focus Report, you'll learn:

  • How subscriptions have the potential to foster stronger resilience for brands in the travel industry

  • Successful experiments in revamping loyalty programs during the pandemic

  • Travelers' loyalty towards airlines and the new role of points

  • How travel, consumer spending, and loyalty intersect in the post-pandemic paradigm
