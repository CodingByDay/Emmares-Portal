We're excited to reveal the 2020 winners of the Skift IDEA Awards!

 

The awards recognize excellence in innovation, design, and experiences defining the future of travel - and this year did not disappoint.

 

We received hundreds of entries from people all over the world for every category including, hospitality innovation, technology, accessibility and inclusion, virtual pivot, sustainability. Each of the winning submissions illustrates how a smart, human-centered approach to design results in more meaningful travel experiences, innovative business models, and sustainable solutions for the future.
