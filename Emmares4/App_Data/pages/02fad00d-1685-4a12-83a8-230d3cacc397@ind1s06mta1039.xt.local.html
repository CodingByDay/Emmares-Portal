Earth will eventually come to an end when the sun swells and dies billions of years from now, but must that mean the end of Earth’s life?

In this talk, Christopher Mason details a 500-year programme that would modify our frail genomes and those of other life forms to tolerate the extremes of outer space – with the ultimate goal of settling new solar systems. Indeed, he argues, as the only species aware of our planet’s fate, humanity has a moral duty to engineer life to reach new worlds.
