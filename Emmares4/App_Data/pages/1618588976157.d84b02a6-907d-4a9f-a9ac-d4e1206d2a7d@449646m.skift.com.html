New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: March 2021 Highlights

March 2021 might go down as a true turning point for the travel industry.

In our latest Skift Recovery Index report, we present some exciting fronts that could lead to a strong summer rebound. Global travel index score in March passed the 50-point mark for the first time since the pandemic began. The U.S., benefiting from its smooth vaccine rollout, registered a whooping 73 score, only 27% below the pre-pandemic performance at the same time period.
