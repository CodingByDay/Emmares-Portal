New Skift Research — U.S. Travel Tracker: July 2021 Highlights

Nearly half of Americans traveled in July, an all-time high since January 2020. However, as the Delta Variant sweeps across the country, the rebound might be short-lived.

In our newly released U.S. Travel Tracker July 2021  report, we highlight key trends in July travel and report the alarming plummet in consumer confidence and future travel intent. 

What You’ll Learn From This Report:

  • Travel incidences, Jan 2020 – July 2021
  • July travel highlights
  • COVID-19 impacted travel, Oct 2020 – July 2021
  • Changing consumer sentiments on the economic outlook, Feb 2020 – Aug 2021
  • Consumer intent for the remainder of 2021 travel
  • Remote work trend and its impact on travel and mobility 
  • How COVID-19 vaccination might impact travel
