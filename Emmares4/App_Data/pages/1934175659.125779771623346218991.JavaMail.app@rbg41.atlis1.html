Dear Reader,

This week, we're wondering whether the truth is out there. "The truth" meaning, of course, whether or not a soon-to-be-released U.S. government report will reveal that a series of unexplained aerial phenomena observed by military personnel are in fact due to alien visitations. Our lead story consults multiple experts for their opinions about the forthcoming report (spoiler alert: most of them doubt the "extraterrestrial hypothesis"). Elsewhere, we have stories about fresh images from the giant moon Ganymede, the possibility of antimatter stars, and the chilling possibility of Earth someday encountering a primordial black hole. All this and more is yours to read below. Enjoy!

Lee Billings, Senior Editor, Space & Physics
