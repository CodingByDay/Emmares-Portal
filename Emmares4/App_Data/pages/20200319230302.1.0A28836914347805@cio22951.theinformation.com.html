How Female Founders Are Treated by the Tech Press

You are invited to a special conversation between Kara Swisher and Jessica Lessin about media coverage surrounding female founders and executives.

The press is scrutinizing private tech companies and their leaders more than ever before. Amid a spate of aggressive coverage, a debate has arisen about whether female founders are being treated fairly by the tech press.

After her column last week, The Information's editor-in-chief Jessica is sitting down with veteran tech reporter and columnist Kara Swisher of Recode and the New York Times to discuss the topic, analyze this current moment and suggest a path forward.

Because this is such an important topic, we have decided to make this call free to the public. This call is limited to 1,000 participants and a recording will be made available to the public afterward.
