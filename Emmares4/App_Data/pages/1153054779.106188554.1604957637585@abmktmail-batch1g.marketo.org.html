Although 92% of CIOs say a “dual-track” approach is essential to digital transformation — pairing traditional, IT-led grand scale transformation with a new form of business-led “rapid cycle innovation” — only 31% of companies are adopting the approach!

 

On Thursday, November 12th, Quick Base VP of Product Management Andrew Varley and Product Manager Alice Rich will discuss how their platform can help dramatically transform your business.


Using traditional, IT-led grand scale transformation, IT and business leaders have created a solid tech foundation and are making some progress, but…

  1. Core customizations are too rigid, expensive, and slow, relying on hard-to-find specialty resources.
  2. Point solution sprawl creates extra work, hundreds of data islands, and mounting expenses.
  3. And more DIY tools, like error-prone spreadsheets and no code tools, make data invisible to employees who need it day-to-day and a burden to maintain.

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN

Unite your data and put it to work

Build an ecosystem of apps that work for your workflow

Safely engage your people in citizen development to let those closest to the work build the solutions
