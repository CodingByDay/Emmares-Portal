Well, dear readers, this week is basically the Super Bowl for the Ars Dealmaster team. Though Black Friday deals and gift guides have long landed across the Internet earlier this month, the nation's retailers are prepped and ready to begin peddling piles of junk to capitalize on the shopping frenzy that takes place this Friday through next (Cyber) Monday. 

 

Make no mistake, those 72 hours also include more legitimately worthwhile discounts on tech than virtually any other point in the year. So our Dealmaster team will be monitoring the special offers and continuously updating a running list of deals onsite both Friday and Monday. (They're also prepping to launch a regular newsletter of tech deals, so subscribe now if you're the routine shopper type.)

 

 

Ars started as a hardcore consumer tech site in 1999, and it remains very much a hardcore consumer tech site at its core in 2019. So for this week's Orbital Transmission, we simply had to offer a bit of guidance on the tech we most recommend gifting in 2019 and the deals we're already taking advantage of before the wave of Black Friday/Cyber Monday email promotions flood your inbox. Remember, you don't need everything that you see over the next few days—you won't use that Wireless Talking Oven/Barbeque Thermometer, promise—but a small amount of research can lead to a great deal of success this shopping season.

From fancy biking pants to jaw-dropping displays, let the Ars staff wishlist inspire you

Maybe your days of holiday letter writing and toy store brochure browsing are long gone, but everyone has a few items they've been eyeing and would love to see under giftwrap soon. We recently polled Ars staffers for their 2019 holiday wishes, and the list can definitely inspire ideas for any Black Friday searchers. Chief among the most aspirational: the Apple Pro Display XDR had our mouth watering upon its summer unveiling, and that LED lust has not gone away despite the insane price tag.

 

These are the best tech gifts to get under $50

The annual danger of gift giving season is cost—it is so, so easy to experience budget creep and all of a sudden spend hundreds on your gifting (especially if the necessary planning did not happen first). But remember, it's the thought that counts, not the price tag. And luckily the Ars' Dealmaster team has been testing and tracking plenty of lovable tech that falls below the $50 threshold. Everyone loves LEGO sets, for instance, and no life hasn't been improved by an external, portable battery.

 

If you favor function, find tech that travels well

We salute you, functional gift giver. You truly can never have enough socks, cash doesn't go bad, and toothbrushes for Halloween aren't really that bad in retrospect. But if you want to deliver both function and fun this holiday season, we'd recommend thinking travel-oriented. Today's tech has evolved to a place where many, many cool things happen to be ultra-portable and forever untied to your office. Some of it is fancy, a lot of it is fairly reasonable. But we have yet to meet someone whose life wouldn't be improved by the ability to have a portable speaker in hand ready with a scenario-appropriate Spotify list.
