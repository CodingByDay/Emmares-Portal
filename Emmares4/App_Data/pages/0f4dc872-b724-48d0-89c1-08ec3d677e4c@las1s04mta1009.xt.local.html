Where adventure awaits

This small paradise island has so much to offer adventure seekers. Hike through statuesque cacti and the rugged desert-like terrain of the Arikok National Park. Explore the hidden gems of the island by quad or horseback. Or discover the rich sea life and mangroves of Mangel Halto by paddleboard, kayak or by diving into the warm crystal clear waters.
