 Dear Reader ,

Over the last year, we’ve seen a big increase in demand for keyboards that connect to an iPad for home learning.

 

All CT-S and LK-S keyboards can currently do this with a USB cable. Now, selected models can also use the NEW WU-BT10 Bluetooth adapter, which makes it even easier to connect an iPad, iPhone or selected Android device*.

This tiny USB dongle adds Bluetooth Audio (so you can use the keyboard as a Bluetooth speaker) AND Bluetooth MIDI.

 

These new features make it possible to enjoy music learning apps, such as Casio’s own Chordana Play without the need for any USB cables.
