New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: January 2021 Highlights

The latest report of the Skift Recovery Index, including data from January 3 to January 30 2021, is now available. 

Travel performance remained fairly stable for the last three months. Global index score for January sat at 44, meaning that the travel industry’s holistic performance was at 44% compared to January 2019

In this first highlights report of 2021, we look ahead and provide our view on how vaccination, testing and health passports might impact the recovery path for the year.
