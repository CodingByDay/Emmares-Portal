TGIF, TicToc readers! Start your weekend in high spirits with these 10 uplifting stories from the past week. 

1. Ready for a road-to-air adventure? With a propeller in the back and wings that fold down, the Aircar is meant to be flown like a regular plane to allow pilots to get behind the wheel without more training.

2. Russia released 50 belugas from a "whale jail." They were kept by oceanographers in a marine containment facility and released in the Japanese Sea.

3. A half-court shot nets free tuition. A University of Nevada student got a year of college education paid for after sinking a half-court shot during the season opener.

4. 14-year-old finds a fix for blind spots while driving. She was awarded $25,000 for creating a prototype that linked a webcam outside a car with a projector that relays a live feed inside.

5. Closing the pay gap in Australian soccer. The country's top women footballers will earn the same as male players after the country’s governing soccer body and the players' union struck a landmark deal. 

6.  A carbon solution for New Zealand? The country passed the Zero Carbon Bill to reduce all greenhouse gases to net zero in the next 50 years.

7. AI uncovers secrets of the Berlin Wall. An AR app shows the transformation of the towering structure, 30 years after it was torn down.

8. These schools are made of plastic litter. UNICEF partnered with Conceptos Plasticos to build the schools with bricks made from plastic waste. 

9.  Amsterdam’s famous canals are getting fizzy. The city is experimenting with an underwater device that creates a barrier of bubbles to catch waste in the waterways. 

10. An epic formation Down Under. 15 women set a record for the most female skydivers to pull off a multi-point formation jump over Jurien Bay in Western Australia. 

Hopefully, you’re in a better mood... Now tell your friends to sign up to receive our newsletter five days a week, and follow us on Twitter, YouTube, Instagram and Facebook.

Thanks for reading!
-Masiel Torres
