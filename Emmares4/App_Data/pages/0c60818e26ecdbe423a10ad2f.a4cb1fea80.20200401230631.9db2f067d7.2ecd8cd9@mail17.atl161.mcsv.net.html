Hi there,

 

Every year, we pick out 100 AI startups that we think have the most transformative potential for our AI 100 list. 

 

Here are some of the AI 100 startups we featured that are already working on Covid-19 initiatives:

  • DarwinAI released an open-source neural network model called COVID-Net in conjunction with the University of Waterloo to help researchers diagnose Covid-19 from x-rays or CT scans

  • Biotech firm Atomwise is using AI to identify compounds that can combat rapidly mutating coronaviruses
  • Canada-based biotech co Cyclica is partnering with the China-based Institute of Materia Medica to leverage AI to identify Covid-19 drug candidates
  • Digital health startup Eko offers an AI-powered stethoscope and cardiac screening platform that can screen for signs of Covid-19 infection
