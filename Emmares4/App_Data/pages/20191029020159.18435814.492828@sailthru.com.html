Chili Cheese Bread Bowls

A perfect dish to serve on Halloween, but also a

simple option for busy weeknights.

Who is ready for a little Halloween fun this holiday? These Chili Cheese Bread Bowls require just a handful of ingredients and a few minutes of prep. The end result? Perfect for a spooky good time, this slow-cooked goodness is sure to warm you from the inside and out.

 

Stir the ground beef and incorporate the chili seasoning mix after tossing in the diced onion, bell pepper, and garlic. Once there's no pink left in the meat, transfer the mixture to a 4-quart slow cooker, then toss in Kroger Diced Tomatoes and Kroger Dark Red Kidney Beans, and cook on high. After two hours, you're left with slow cooked hearty goodness that can be easily ladled into your cheesy bread bowls. Toss in a handful of croutons and minced parsley, and you're set!
