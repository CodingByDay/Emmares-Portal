LOCUSTS STORMS INTO CINEMAS OCTOBER 17!!!!

"...this slippery outback thriller is inventively shot and highly entertaining...with stellar performances..." FilmInk

 
There’s no place like home! When high flying tech entrepreneur Ryan Black returns to his remote hometown of Serenity Crossing for his father’s funeral, old family tensions are reignited as he’s reunited with his wise-cracking ex-con brother, Tyson. But when Ryan becomes the target of a deadly extortion scam at the hands of desperate small-town criminals – played by Steve Le Marquand, Justin Rosniak, Ryan Morgan, Alan Dukes and the late, great Damian Hill in this final screen role – the dark underbelly of the sleepy mining town is exposed.
Written and produced by Angus Watts, directed by Heath Davis (Broke, Book Week), and starring Ben Geurens (Reign, Winners & Losers) as Ryan, Nathaniel Dean (Somersault, Candy, Alien: Covenant) as Tyson, and Malcolm Kennard as their violent, abusive father, plus a star turn from Jessica McNamee (The Meg, coming up in Mortal Kombat), Locusts is a stylish outback thriller that will have you on the edge of your seat trying to unravel the many twists and turns along the way.
