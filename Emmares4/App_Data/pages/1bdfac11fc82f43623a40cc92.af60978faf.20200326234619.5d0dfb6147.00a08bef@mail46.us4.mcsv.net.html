Australia’s Premier Online Movie Magazine

While the cinemas might be closed - yes, closed...we can't quite believe it either - we've still got a whole bunch of quality movie reading for you this week! There are early reviews of The Personal History Of David Copperfield and The Hunt, along with interviews with Armando Iannucci; the team behind Lost Girls; and Kelly's Hollywood director Brian Donovan. We also offer up some in-your-face Coronavirus viewing, and revisit Steven Soderbergh's Contagion. Stay safe, and (figuratively if not literally) see you at the movies!

The Full Corona: Non-Escapist Home Viewing
If you don't want to self-isolate and hide away from the world with transporting feel-good flicks, we've got a whole Hazmat suit full of in-your-face virus movies, from Contagion and Outbreak through to The Omega Man and Pontypool. 

Arka Das: Coming Up In Mulan

With a role in the currently on-pause live action Disney remake Mulan, young Aussie actor Arka Das will hopefully soon be a major name to watch. 
Amy Ryan: Lost Girls
The cast and crew of the acclaimed Netflix drama Lost Girls talk about this true life story about a mother seeking justice for her murdered daughter.

Living Kelly's Hollywood

Director Brian Donovan and his wife Tempany Deckert discuss his deeply personal doco Kelly's Hollywood, which follows the life of his sister.
Armando Iannucci: David Copperfield

Opinion: Give Gal A Break
