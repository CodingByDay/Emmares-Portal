Dear Reader,

This week, we're offering an informal summer refresher course on black holes. First up is a new video in our "Decoded" series, about the basic science of these mysterious objects. After watching, turn to some of our associated stories, such as a piece on the possibility of celestial travel via wormholes (black holes' close theoretical cousins), or the latest updates on astronomers' ongoing efforts to magnetically map and even make movies of the chaotic environments immediately surrounding these cosmic monsters. Read on, enjoy, and have a great Memorial Day weekend!

Lee Billings, Senior Editor, Space & Physics
