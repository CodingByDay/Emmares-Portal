A few weeks ago, on the day I was set to host a dinner party, I found myself wandering around my apartment attempting to scrape together enough chairs for the guests. Once it became abundantly clear that even pooling my seating from every room would still not cut it, I did what any desperate host would do: I headed to the discount store.

 

At my local store, I found what I was looking for—kind of. The good news? The store had stools, the kind that folded flat, perfect for storing in a small apartment (Iike these). The bad news? They were pretty ugly. But desperate times call for desperate measures, so I snagged a few and took them home. Then, I set about turning the basic stools into—well, something that doesn't look like it came from the dollar store. Here's how I did it.

WHAT I DID

STEP 1: Remove legs 

Using a screwdriver or a drill, remove the metal legs from your stool.

STEP 2: Spray paint
Now for the fun part: spray paint! With the stool legs open on a covered surface, spray them from the top and sides. You may want to let the paint dry, then flip the legs over and paint from the other side to ensure full coverage.
