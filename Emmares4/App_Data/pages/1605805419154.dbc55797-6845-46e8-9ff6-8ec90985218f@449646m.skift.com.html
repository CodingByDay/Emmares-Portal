New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: October Highlights

The latest report of the Skift Recovery Index, including data from October 4 to October 31, is now available. 

We saw an initial uptick of performance in early October, but then a steep decline at the end of the month which has continued into November. The decline is mostly driven by stricter lockdowns in Europe, with the region in the middle of a second wave.  By the last week of October, the global travel index score stalled at 44, indicating a 56% of performance reduction against the same week in 2019.

In this month’s highlight report, we dive deeper into the performance in key travel markets as well as main travel sectors.
