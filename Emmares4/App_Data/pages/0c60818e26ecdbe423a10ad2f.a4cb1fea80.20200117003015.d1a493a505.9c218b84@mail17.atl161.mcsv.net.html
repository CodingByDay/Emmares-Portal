Say again?

 

Here's the explanation of the tech terms mentioned above. 

  • Slaughterbots are micro-drones that have the power to carry out targeted assassinations. 
  • Quantum supremacy refers to using quantum computers to quickly solve a problem that would take conventional computers an ungainly amount of time to tackle.
  • Grinders are biohackers who implant microchips in their hands or elsewhere in their bodies to continuously track indicators like glucose levels.

One More Thing
Source: Kotchakorn Voraakhom/Landprocess

A Bangkok university recently opened Asia's largest rooftop farm to tackle problems like food security, sustainable energy, urban heat-island effect, and more.

 

Located at Thammasat University, the 236,000-square-foot roof was designed to prevent the flood-prone city from sinking, and is able to hold up to 3M gallons of water in detention ponds. 

 

The farm will also be able to grow enough rice for 100,000+ meals to be served on campus in a year. Any leftovers will be composted to fertilize the next crop of rice.
