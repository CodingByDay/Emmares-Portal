Who’s hiring, how to work remotely, and how to navigate the new normal
We’re Here to Help
If you’re struggling to maintain a sense of normalcy right now, we get it. We’re right here with you.

The way we all approach our daily lives has had to change significantly in a very short period of time—and the world of work has been turned upside down.

We’ll be the first to admit that it’s hard to predict what comes next. But the one thing we know for certain is that we will be here to support you through the challenges ahead. Our mission has always been to create a safe space and a community that you can rely on for career advice and guidance. Now, more than ever, we want to help you keep growing as individuals and professionals as we navigate the unknown together.

We’ve compiled a list of companies that are still actively hiring and we’re updating it frequently to keep you informed of available opportunities.

We’re creating new resources to help you figure out what to do if your job has been affected, manage anxiety around COVID-19, effectively work from home for the foreseeable future, and more.

We’ll also continue to share the great job search and career advice you’ve always come to us for—because no matter what you need in your work life right now, we’re here for you.

Remember: We’re in this together.


Warmly,
Kathryn Minshew, founder of The Muse
