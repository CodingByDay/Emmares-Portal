Visual Of The Week

We recently published the annual AI 100, our list of the 100 most promising AI startups in the world. Healthcare was the most heavily featured category in our list and is the top industry for AI deal activity at large. 

 

Since publishing, a number of companies on the list have been working to combat and contain Covid-19. Startups like DarwinAI and Eko are facilitating faster and more precise diagnosis, while Atomwise and Cyclica are using AI to discover potential drug compound candidates to fight the virus.

Source: CB Insights

7 of the companies in the cohort have struck new deals since publishing, the most recent being AI systems developer 4Paradigm. The company raised $85M in a second tranche of its Series C last week and is now valued at $2B. 4Paradigm has recently shifted to develop solutions for precision screening and contact sourcing of Covid-19.
