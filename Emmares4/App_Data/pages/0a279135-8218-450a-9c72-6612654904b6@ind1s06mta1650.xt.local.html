Outer Peace Episode 7 - Mikhail Martin

Mikhail is challenging the view that black people don’t climb. As co-founder of Brothers of Climbing, he’s dedicated to opening up the outdoors to people of colour. By countering preconceptions of climbing, Mikhail hopes to increase minority involvement and inspire new generations to come together in nature as a community.

“The biggest problem in the world right now is that people don't understand each other and even worse, they don't seem to care to try. We have the ability right now to be curious, question and enact change. But it will take a coordinated effort, a community.”

Mikhail Martin, co-founder of Brothers of Climbing
