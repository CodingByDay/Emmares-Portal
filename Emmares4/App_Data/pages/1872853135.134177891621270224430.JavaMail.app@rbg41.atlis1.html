May 17, 2021

Dear Reader,

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention announced last week that vaccinated people no longer need to wear masks or social distance in most indoor or outdoor settings—a major step toward a sense of normalcy, but one that some critics say may be premature. Adolescents are now eligible to get the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID vaccine. And a groundbreaking trial showed the psychoactive drug MDMA—when paired with extensive therapy—is effective in treating post-traumatic stress disorder.

Tanya Lewis, Senior Editor, Health & Medicine
