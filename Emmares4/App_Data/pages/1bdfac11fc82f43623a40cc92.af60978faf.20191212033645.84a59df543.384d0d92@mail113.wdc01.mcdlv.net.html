THE LATEST
Sir Ian McKellen Is The Good Liar
The British legend and star of blockbusters like X-Men and The Lord Of The Rings delivers one of his finest performances in years in the delightfully labyrinthine thriller The Good Liar, opposite the equally essential Helen Mirren. 

Niels Tavernier: The Ideal Palace

French actor turned director Nils Tavernier (The Finishers) tells an extraordinary true story of one man's amazing achievement with The Ideal Palace. 
Simon Baker: In Conversation
FilmInk sits down with veteran Aussie actor Simon Baker at The Marrakech Film Festival for a chat about his life, his career, and his approach to film.

Lily James: On The Rise

With impressive credits including Cinderella, Baby Driver, Mamma Mia 2, Yesterday and Downton Abbey, Lily James is on the cusp of something big.
Suho: From K-Pop To The Big Screen
A huge pop star in his native Korea, teen sensation Kuho (leader of the group Exo) is now making the move to the big screen with a host of films. 

Miah Madden: Life Changing Films

The exciting young star of The Unlisted, Mustangs FC, Australia Day and The Gods Of Wheat Street talks about the films that changed her life.
Sue Maslin: The Future Of Film
The innovative and industrious producer behind The Dressmaker and Japanese Story provides an insight into the changing face of Australian film.
