Every year at the beginning of January, the Gadget Lab crew packs up and heads to Las Vegas for CES, the annual tech industry gathering. It's a massive show—miles upon miles of expo hall booth space occupied by thousands of companies showing off their newest gadgets.

We did our best to see as much of CES as we could this week, and we've selected the 11 items that stood out from the fray. These are the items that are most innovative, surprising, and smartly designed. Check out our list of the Best of CES 2020. Also be sure to see our three days of photo galleries from the show floor.

Also this week, CES welcomed sex tech for the first time, WIRED editor-in-chief Nick Thompson interviewed Twitter's head of product, and Lauren Goode and Louise Matsakis wrote about the ongoing controversy with Amazon and Ring's participation in community policing.
