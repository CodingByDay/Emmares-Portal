Audi A6 plugs in, Alfa GTA breaks free
Mature, sensible and pragmatic - all words that can describe the Audi A6 50 TFSi e quattro in this week's review. Just shy of 300bhp, yet just 36g/km CO2, could it be the ultimate company car for those who don't want to follow the herd into SUVs?

Few of those words can be applied to the Alfa Romeo Giulia GTAm; we drive the track-focused - but not necessarily 'hardcore' Alfa super saloon and get in touch with its roots in this week's video.

Plus, we look at the only electric estate car you can buy in the UK - the MG5 - and test the Audi e-Tron GT before catching up with the latest news from Volkswagen, Tesla and Ineos.

Enjoy, and have a great weekend
