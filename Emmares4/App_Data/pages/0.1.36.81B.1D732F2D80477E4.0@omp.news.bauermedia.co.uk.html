Turn it up: 508 Sport Engineered, M4 CSL, Golf R
Excitement, passion and adrenaline sell cars - if they didn't we'd all be driving Dacia Sanderos. Even electric cars are getting packaged with 'hot' models, but let's face it - you need more than just stickers & spoilers to deliver that visceral appeal. Hence, Peugeot's 508 Peugeot Sport Engineered - the French firm's most exciting executive car for the UK since the 405 Mi16x4.

It arrives just as BMW is caught preparing the hardcore M4 CSL and Golf Rs start to take to the streets; we put the fast VW up against rivals from BMW and Mercedes in this week's video.

Plus, catch the latest Hyundai concepts - including a very retro Pony - see what's next for Ferrari's EV strategy, and find out what the Skoda Enyaq is like as Audi announces its MEB offering.

Enjoy, and thanks for reading!
