We’re looking forward to the Skift Short-Term Rental & Outdoor Summit taking place from December 9-10. Here are the questions we'll address.

Skift Short-Term Rental & Outdoor Summit is happening December 9-10. Register now to secure your spot and hear from founders and CEOs at Vrbo, Marriott International, G Adventures, KOA, RVshare, Under Canvas, and more.

 

Our agenda will be announced in the coming week, and here are just a few of the big questions we are planning to address across two-days of insightful and engaging talks:

  • How has the Short-Term Rental shifted? Skift Research will present new findings and analysis. 
  • What are the opportunities for both short-term rentals and outdoor travel to capture growth out of this crisis? 
  • What will online travel agencies do to help standardize and provide assurance to travelers through a fragmented STR ecosystem? 
  • With a dramatic rise in demand, will outdoor travel companies become more sophisticated and modernize the sector?
  • How are investors viewing the landscape as the industry both professionalizes and grows?
  • Will the tech stack be more readily adopted by independent owners as they seek streamlined operations and drive growth? 

 

December 9th will focus on Short-Term Rentals and December 10th will discuss the Outdoor Travel sector. Join us for this exciting online event as we bring together industry leaders for frank and transparent conversations on travel's future.

 

Can’t attend live? Recordings and presentations will be available on-demand for registered attendees after the Forum.

 

We hope to see you there. For any questions, email forum@skift.com.
