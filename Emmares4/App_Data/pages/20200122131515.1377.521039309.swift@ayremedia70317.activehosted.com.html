Sentiment remains high
 

The Story

Despite a recent health scare hitting the Chinese mainland, the general sentiment for the upcoming Chinese New Year (CNY) holiday at the end of this month remains high and authorities with the Macau International Airport (MIA) announced that it would see an additional 120 flights during the CNY celebrations. Meanwhile, media outlet GGRAsia reported that the majority of luxury hotels in the Cotai region of Macau are already sold out. 14 of the 19 venues, including mostly five-star properties, are completely full for at least four days of the period, which will run January 24 through January 30. The CNY officially starts on January 25.
