Secret #2: The Power of Fast, Fun, and Easy Lessons



The best way to fail at anything is to overwhelm yourself.

Sitting in a 2-hour class. Reading 50 pages of a dry, boring textbook. Using an app for 1 hour. All of these will leave you tired and frustrated. The result? You don’t want to go through that again. So you quit. The end.

The best way to succeed? Start small and learn with something you enjoy.

Secret #3: The Power of Small & Simple Routines



Just like a 3-15 minute lesson won’t overwhelm you today…

...a small and simple routine won’t overwhelm you in the long run.

Examples of bad routines:
  • Reading 2-3 chapters a day.
  • Studying for 5 hours a day.
  • Memorizing 100 words a day.
Examples of good routines:
  • Do 1 lesson a day (for 5 days of the week).
  • Spend 5-10 minutes on learning Korean a day.
Yes, it can be as simple as that.

Day after day, week after week, you learn and speak MORE and MORE Korean.
  • To do now: Create a routine.
For example: Do 1 lesson a day for 5 days of the week. Saturday and Sunday are off.
