A legend returns: Countach LPI 800-4
We didn't think it would happen, and rumours of a revived name on an electric SUV served only to spark controversy - but the new Countach is here. Lamborghini has resurrected the '70s legend, but this is no necromantic notion or restomod one-off - it's true to the spirit, but as modern as Lamborghini's last combustion engined models can be.

With everything going on in the world, it's almost as controversial as if it had been that SUV; a self-indulgent, decadent wedge-shaped vision, and we're here for it.

Join in the indulgence of this limited-edition nostalgia trip as we relive some of the moments Lamborghini and CAR have shared on the route between the original Countach, and the LPI 800-4. From LJK Setright's 1000-mile epic drive in a Muira, to the latest, track-honed Huracan.

If the Countach is all too nostalgic for you, we've got the latest future vision from Audi, a comment on cars in the form of Paul Smith's latest Mini, and the newest reality from BMW as well.

Enjoy, and have a great weekend!
