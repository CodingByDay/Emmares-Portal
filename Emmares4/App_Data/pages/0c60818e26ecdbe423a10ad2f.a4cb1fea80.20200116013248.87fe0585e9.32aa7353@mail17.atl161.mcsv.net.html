The vibes that bind

Hi there,

 

Dating in 2020 is wild.

 

Yesterday we talked about the billionaire who’s looking for a life partner to travel to the moon with him.

 

But if you’re stuck on Earth and in a long-distance relationship, this one’s for you. 

 

You can now buy pairs of electronic bracelets that send light vibrations and a blink of colored light to let your partner know that you’re thinking about them. The bracelets are connected to an app that shows info about your significant other’s time zone, weather, etc.

 

The goal of the bracelets was initially to simulate random gestures of affection from far away, but users say they’ve become more of a method to communicate when they don’t have access to their phone.
