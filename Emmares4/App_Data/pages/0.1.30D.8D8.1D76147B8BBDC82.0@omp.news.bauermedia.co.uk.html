The London Classic Car Show is taking place next weekend, on the weekend of the 25th – 27th June at Syon Park.
 
Over 120 Everyman Classics will be crossing the block and for the first time since 2019, you will be able to safely join CCA in the room and bid in person. Alternatively, if you’re unable to attend in person you can bid online, by phone or via commission (pre-bid) and the sale will also be live streamed on YouTube.
 
Some of the catalogue can be previewed below, with the full lot list available on the CCA website here.
 
To register to bid please contact the CCA team on 01926 640888, by email (bid@classiccarauctions.co.uk) or via their website.
