Lose Yourself (and Your Cares) in Nature’s Symphony

 

We all know how hard the pandemic has made it to just get outside into the open air, so we thought we would bring the great outdoors to you. Introducing National Park Nature Walks, our new pop-up podcast series. Host Jacob Job, an ecologist and audiophile, brings you inches away from a multitude of creatures, great and small, amid the sonic grandeur of nature. The 5-part series is a birder’s paradise. You’ll hear Ruby-Crowned Kinglets, American Bitterns, Ruffed Grouse, Warbling Vireos, Townsend's Solitaires, Siskins, Wilson's Snipes, and so many more.
 

Take this acoustic journey, and you’ll feel like you’re virtually back outside again.
