Hash Brown Mini Quiches

Make-ahead bites perfect for holiday brunches

The holiday season is high time for special brunch plans. Nothing says merry and bright like your loved ones gathered in the kitchen, enjoying a cozy breakfast. Whether you're hosting or playing the guest, these make-ahead mini quiches are the the perfect passable bite to share.

 

Whisk up a blend of eggs, shredded cheese, and shredded hash browns until combined, and season with plenty of salt and pepper. To build the base of your mini quiches, scoop the mixture and press into a well-oiled baking tin. While those bake, fry up a batch of Kroger® Traditional Pork Sausage patties until browned, then chop into bite-sized pieces. To create the eggy filling, combine the sausage bites with onions, whisked eggs, heavy cream, and baby spinach for a hit of color. Complete the quiche by adding the egg mixture to the hash brown cups and baking until set and perfectly golden.
