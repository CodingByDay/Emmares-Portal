The Story

The Nevada Gaming Control Board (NGCB) did not give credence to the defense made by Steve Wynn that the regulator has no power to prosecute him. Wynn claims that the NGCB cannot file a case against him because he has already resigned from the company in February 2018 and has sold his shares in the company. The NGCB rejected these claims, stating the acts did not strip the regulator of its "full and absolute power" to recommend that the state Gaming Commission impose sanctions on Steve for casting the state’s gaming sector in a negative light.
