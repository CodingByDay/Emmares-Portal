What is the aim of this survey?

To investigate the prevalence, type, frequency and triggers for dizziness, unsteadiness and/or falls and their impact on daily activities, work, and emotional state in adults 18-95 years’ old who have had a positive COVID-19 test. 

Who can complete the survey?

Persons aged 18-95 years’ old who have had a positive COVID-19 test and experienced dizziness, unsteadiness and or falls. 

   What the participation will include?

Participants are asked to complete a 10-minute online anonymous survey. The anonymised research data will be made available publicly to help provide information on prevalence and impact of dizziness, unsteadiness and/or falls in adults who have had COVID-19. This may lead to better understanding of appropriate assessment protocols and management strategies for persons who have had COVID-19 and experienced dizziness, unsteadiness and/or falls.
