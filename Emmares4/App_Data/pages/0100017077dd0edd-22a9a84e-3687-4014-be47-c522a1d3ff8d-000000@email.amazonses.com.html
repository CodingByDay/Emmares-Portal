We are writing this mail to inform you about KGSP Graduate Scholarship 2020 announcement. 

For the year 2020, NIIED Korea is inviting 1276 international students from 153 countries, for degree (Master's & Doctoral) and non-degree research programs. 

KGSP is one of the most prestigious scholarships in Korea. It provides selected students two-way airfare, settlement allowance, one year Korean language training, 100% university fee as well as monthly living expenses.

You can apply for this scholarship - through Korean embassy in your country or directly to the affiliated universities in Korea. 

Knowing Korean language is not compulsory to apply for KGSP scholarship but if you have TOPIK certificate it is an advantage.  For the eligibility criteria, application guidelines, application forms, university information and FAQ, go to the following page and refer to the attached files.

NIIED Korean Government Scholarship Program (KGSP) 2020

*Application deadline may differ from one country/institution to another but it is expected to fall sometime in March.
