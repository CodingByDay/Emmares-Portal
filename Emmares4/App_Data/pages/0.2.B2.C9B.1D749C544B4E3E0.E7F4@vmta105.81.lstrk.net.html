A traditional Toro cigar is a notch above Robusto cigars when it comes to size. You can almost always find a toro size under any cigar brand's portfolio. It is one of the easiest sizes to make. Standing at 6 to 6 1/2 inches in length, toro cigars usually have a ring gauge from 52 to 54. Typically, a Toro will burn and last you forty-five minutes, to over an hour.
