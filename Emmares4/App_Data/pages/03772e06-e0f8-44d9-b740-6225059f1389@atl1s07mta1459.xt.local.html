The beauty of pre-NFL draft Fantasy mocks is that our experts draft based on player talent rather than fit. This is more important in dynasty start-ups than anything, which is why the results of our most recent Dynasty Superflex start-up mock are very telling. For example, Tua Tagovailoa was drafted as the QB10, just ahead of Matt Ryan and Baker Mayfield, which shows how much upside Tagovailoa has, regardless of landing spot. Also, Jonathan Taylor led the group of 45 rookies drafted, but how far did the rest of the rookie RBs and WRs fall?

On today's podcast, we spoke at length about the 2020 rookie WR class, which looks to be one of the strongest in recent memory. How do Lamb and Jeudy compare to youngsters A.J. Brown and Michael Gallup? Which sleeper WR prospects could be relevant in Fantasy this season?
