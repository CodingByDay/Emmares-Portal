Happy Absolutely Not Tuesday! (Surprise: It’s Wednesday)

As RG’s EIC, I have to make the important decisions. What we publish, when we publish it, who writes it—all that good stuff. So when we got the opportunity to review a remote controlled airplane and Josh said his son would love it, I make a call: why doesn’t Josh get his son to write the review?

And that’s exactly what happened. Josh helped his son a little bit and only edited for spelling. The end result is probably my favorite thing we’ve ever published: a very lightly edited review straight from the mind of an eight-year-old. I love it.

Other highlights:

  • Prime Day is coming. Amazon’s biggest sale of the year is happening on June 21-22.
  • Apple TV is now available on Android TV. It was already on Chromecast, but now SHIELD and all the others can enjoy it too. Very nice.
  • You can get a Raspberry Pi chip for just a dollar. You can’t even buy a soda that cheap.
  • Wyze opened pre-ordered for another vacuum cleaner. This time it’s a stick vac. And it looks, uh, pretty good?
  • …and so much more!
