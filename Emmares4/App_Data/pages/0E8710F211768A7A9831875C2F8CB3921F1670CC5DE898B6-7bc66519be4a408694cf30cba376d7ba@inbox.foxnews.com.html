• Dow jumps 200 points led by Chevron, oil at $65 level

    U.S. stocks kicked off the week mixed as the Dow Jones Industrial Average grabbed a fresh record with Chevron and chemical maker Dow leading the pack. While the S&P 500 was little changed, energy companies jumped over 2% as investors gauged the potential impact of the Colonial Pipeline attack, the largest fuel transport system from the Gulf Coast up to the East Coast. The Nasdaq Composite fell over 1% as the rotation from tech names picked up steam.
