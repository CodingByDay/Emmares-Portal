2019: The Year In Review
From The Irishman through to Aladdin, we collate all of FilmInk's reviews for 2019, with links to each and every review that we uploaded this year. 2019's biggest surprises? Only one film copped a coveted $20 rating, and there was not one single $0 "awarded".
