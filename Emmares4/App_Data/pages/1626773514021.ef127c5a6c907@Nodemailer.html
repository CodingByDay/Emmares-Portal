2013: American journalist Helen Thomas, who broke through a number of barriers for women reporters and was known especially for her coverage of U.S. presidents, died in Washington, D.C. [Sort fact from fiction in our quiz about famous Americans.]

1968: At Chicago's Soldier Field, the first Special Olympics began, and some 1,000 athletes—all of whom had intellectual disabilities—participated. [Test your knowledge of sports.]

1976: The Viking 1 lander touched down at Chryse Planitia on Mars. [Sort fact from fiction in our quiz about Mars.]
