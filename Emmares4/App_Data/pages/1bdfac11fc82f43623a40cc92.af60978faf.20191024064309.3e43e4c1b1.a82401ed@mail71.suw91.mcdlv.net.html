Antoinette Iesue Heads Down The Aisle With Promised
Charming and talented, young Australian actress Antoinette Iesue is right at the heart of the new bittersweet comedy drama Promised, an inside look at arranged marriages and the Italian-Australian diaspora of the 1950s and 1960s.

Andie MacDowell: Ready Or Not

The veteran star of Four Weddings And A Funeral and Groundhog Day (to name just two) takes it to the edge in the horror flick Ready Or Not. 
Samara Weaving: Ready Or Not
Poised for big things, young Australian actress on the rise Samara Weaving is front and centre in the talking point horror film Ready Or Not.

After The Wedding: Complex Emotions
