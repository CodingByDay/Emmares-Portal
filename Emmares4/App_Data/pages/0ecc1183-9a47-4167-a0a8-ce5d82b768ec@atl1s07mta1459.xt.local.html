2020 will be a very interesting season for the quarterback position in Fantasy, as there are plenty of QBs in the back end of their careers who could be top 10 options (see: Drew Brees and Tom Brady), while many young QBs have upside to finish in the top 3. In our latest mock draft, which included a Superflex spot, both Patrick Mahomes and Lamar Jackson were selected in round 1, and 13 QBs were drafted by the end of round 7. A few QB values were majorly impacted by free agency, as we saw Josh Allen jump to round 4, Baker Mayfield rise to round 8, and Deshaun Watson fall to round 4.

Plus, the FFT crew is preparing you for the QBs of the future with a QB Prospect Profiles podcast episode. Is Joe Burrow worth picking in the first round of rookie-only drafts? Can Tua be Fantasy-relevant this season? And who has the most upside outside of Burrow and Tua?
