We’re publishing a new trend report, in partnership with TripActions: New Report: The State of Business Travel 2020. The report investigates the evolving industry trends, technology, and traveler habits shaping business travel in the year ahead.

The role that business travel plays for employees and companies is more important than ever before. But many of the longstanding assumptions that once informed the spending and policy decisions of industry decision-makers are falling by the wayside. In place of these assumptions are new rules: Technology is reshaping how business travelers plan and book their trips, employee habits and expectations are evolving, and new industry competitors are bringing fresh ideas and options to corporate travel buyers.

Skift partnered with TripActions on a research study analyzing the attitudes and opinions of corporate travel managers and business travelers this forms the basis of this report.
