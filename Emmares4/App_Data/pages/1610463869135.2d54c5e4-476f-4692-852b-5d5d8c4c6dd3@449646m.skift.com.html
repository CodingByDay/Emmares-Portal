We’re publishing a new report, in partnership with Medallia Zingle: Five Contactless Hotel Technology Trends for 2021, that shows how hotels are increasingly turning to contactless technology. Many in the industry are finding it not only reduces friction for guests and meets customers on devices and channels where they spend most of their time, but also creates new, unlikely ways to communicate with and serve travelers during the Covid-19 era and beyond.

  • Interviews with hotel executives about how they’re deploying contactless solutions to better serve guests and help their staff
