As the ICE ban approaches we're heading for a land of confusion; on the one hand, we've got electric vehicles such as the new Bentley EV and Megane E-Tech dominating development, and on the other - Hyundai's launching its American-centric luxury brand Genesis over here. Will the G80 and GX80 sell in England by the pound, or is this just the fading lights of the internal-combustion era?

While counting out time on ICE, we review the Continental GT Speed on-track, and the 2021 Mini John Cooper Works on the road, for the best of British thrills.
