Trend Of The Week

Now that businesses are beginning to reopen, hygiene is one of the hottest topics on corporate earnings calls.

 

From the Fortune 500 to restaurant chains to cannabis dispensaries, our earnings call mentions tool shows a  surge in chatter from businesses considering how to maintain proper hygiene while welcoming back employees and customers.

Corporate earnings call mentions of “sanitation” and “disinfection” have skyrocketed in Q2’20.

Source:  CB Insights

In addition to measures like limiting traffic to outdoor spaces or requiring face masks, many are looking at ways to keep spaces clean while reducing human touchpoints.

 

One promising avenue in this regard is self-cleaning solutions, which can automatically and continuously sanitize surfaces by destroying or inhibiting the growth of germs. We profiled  9 startups developing self-cleaning solutions in our brief here.
