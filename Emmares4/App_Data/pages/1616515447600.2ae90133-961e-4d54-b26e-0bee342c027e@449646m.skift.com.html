New Skift Research — Sustainability in Travel 2021: Emissions Benchmark of Six Global Hotel Companies

At an estimated 21% of total travel emissions, the accommodation sector is a considerable contributor to climate change. Room capacity and hotel pipelines have registered strong growth over the past decade, adding to the industry’s footprint.

In Skift Research’s newly released “Sustainability in Travel 2021: Emissions Benchmark of Six Global Hotel Companies”, we compare carbon emissions of six largest hotel companies worldwide, pinpoint the main barriers to cross-comparing performances, and provide solutions for improvement.

What You'll Learn From This Report:

  • The hotel company leaders and laggards in emissions reduction
  • The targets for emission reduction set by the six largest hotel companies, and the shortcomings of these targets
  • How chain scales have a major impact on company emissions, and how these chain scales can be used to provide a benchmark for company performance
  • The future challenges of continued emissions reduction
  • Shortcomings of current reporting standards, and how this could be improved
