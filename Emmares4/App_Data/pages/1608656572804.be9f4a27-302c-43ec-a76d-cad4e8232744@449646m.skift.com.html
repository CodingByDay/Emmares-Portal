New Skift Research — Skift Research Global Travel Outlook 2021

Skift Research’s annual Travel Outlook report is here.

With much uncertainty ahead of us in 2021, we have decided to take a different approach to our outlook report. Instead of forecasting travel market losses and gains, we present key themes that we believe will shape travel recovery in 2021 and beyond.

What You'll Learn From This Report:

  • An uneven global economic recovery
  • Travel in 2021 could look more normal than many expect
  • Leisure outperforms business…
  • … But the reports of business travel’s demise are greatly exaggerated
  • Short-term rentals in the spotlight
  • Capital is plentiful, driving both innovation and consolidation
  • While brands consolidate, regions fragment
  • The distribution pendulum swings back
  • What will look different when travel recovers?
  • Travel will return!
