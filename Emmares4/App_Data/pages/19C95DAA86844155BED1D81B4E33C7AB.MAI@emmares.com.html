Strike partners

When two strikers play together they develop an understanding of what each other is doing – therefore more chances are created. This session concentrates on the forwards then integrates defenders and team mates to replicate a real match...MORE

Reception class

Receiving the ball is more than just a case of simply controlling it when it reaches a player. He has to make sure the first touch is into space so it gives him time to make the next decision, whether that be a pass, shot or dribble...MORE
