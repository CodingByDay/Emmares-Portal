Dear Reader,

Did you know that the FBI uses a dilated pupil as a marker of deception? But one of the most intriguing findings about pupil size is that it may reveal something about a person's intelligence, as gauged by tests of reasoning, attention and memory. Researchers at Georgia Institute of Technology discovered that a larger baseline pupil size holds a correlation with things like fluid intelligence. 

Gary Stix, Senior Editor, Mind & Brain
