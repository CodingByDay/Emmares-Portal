THE LATEST
Constance Wu Has All The Right Moves In Hustlers
Breakout talent Constance Wu stole her scenes with aplomb in Crazy Rich Asians, and now she goes toe to toe with Jennifer Lopez in the surprise hit, Hustlers, which sees a crew of strippers take down their toxic Wall Street clients. 

Fleabag: Where It All Began

With Fleabag, Killing Eve, and her major part in the development of the next James Bond film, Phoebe Waller-Bridge is one of film and TV's hottest talents.
Samara Weaving: Ready Or Not
The niece of the great Hugo Weaving continues her upwards trajectory as an actress with the leading role in the acclaimed horror flick Ready Or Not.

From Footy Field To Film Screen

Best known for his bruising antics on the footy field, NRL star and British import Rabbitoh George Burgess now takes to the screen in Locusts.
The Kelly Gang Rides Straight To Stan
In a pointer to how film distribution has changed, the highly anticipated True History Of The Kelly Gang has been picked up for streaming by Stan.
