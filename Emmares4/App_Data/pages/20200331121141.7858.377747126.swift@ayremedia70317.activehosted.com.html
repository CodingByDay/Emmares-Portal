No hint of when to re-open
 

The Story

Cambodian Prime Minister Hun Sen has announced that all casinos will be ordered to close on April 1, in an effort to help fight COVID-19. The decision came as Cambodia’s number of infected reached over 100 cases. No national lockdown or state of emergency has been announced, but the latter is being considered. The Prime Minister gave no hint as to when casinos may be allowed to re-open, instead suggesting it very much depends on the situation.
