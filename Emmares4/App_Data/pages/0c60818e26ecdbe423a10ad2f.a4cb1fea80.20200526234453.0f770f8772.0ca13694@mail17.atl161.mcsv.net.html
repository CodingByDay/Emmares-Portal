Don't be a hoarder

Hi there, 

 

Pandemic purchases are turning into a whole niche of their own. People have gone rabid buying yeast starters, Nintendo Switches, etc. 

 

Now the VCs have caught on and gone wild. 

 

In Q1’20, investors concentrated their bets on food, gaming, and fitness startups, notching mega-rounds like Impossible Foods’ $500M Series F and mobile game studio Scopely’s $200M Series D.

Meanwhile, fintech broadly saw dollars and deals plunge to their worst levels in 2 years — investors pulled back from payments, which saw funding and deals plummet after a soaring Q4, and insurtech, which saw funding drop to a 5-quarter low.

 

Ouch.
