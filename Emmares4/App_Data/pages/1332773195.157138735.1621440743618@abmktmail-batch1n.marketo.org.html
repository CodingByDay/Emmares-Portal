China's autonomous vehicle startups are coming to TC Mobility 2021

The AV funding spree continues to exhilarate China’s robotaxi industry with startups raising hundreds of millions of dollars over the past year.

 

Will investors continue to back the lofty dreams and skyrocketing valuations of China’s robotaxi leaders? And how will the autonomous driving race play out differently from that in the U.S?

 

We hope to find out on June 9 at TC Sessions: Mobility with 3 leaders from the Chinese autonomous vehicle startup scene: 

 

Jewel Li

COO, AutoX

AutoX is operating the 1st and only fully driverless RoboTaxi service to the general public in China. Before joining AutoX, Dr Li received her PhD in Computer Science and was a senior engineer at IBM.

 

Huan Sun

GM of Europe, Momenta

Sun has led Momenta's efforts in commercializing autonomous driving technology with the mission of Momenta — Better AI, Better Life. Sun received her master's at MIT.

 

Jennifer Li

VP of Finance, WeRide

WeRide is the first self-driving company to obtain strategic investment from top-tier global automakers as well as the first startup in the world holding driverless test permits in both China and the US.

 

The industry is still years from being truly driverless and operational at scale, and we can't wait to hear from this panel as we delve into further understanding the AV industry in China as it continues to expand and change the industry landscape.
