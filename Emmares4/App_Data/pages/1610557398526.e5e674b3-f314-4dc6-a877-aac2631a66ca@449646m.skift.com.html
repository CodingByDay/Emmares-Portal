New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: December Highlights

The latest report of the Skift Recovery Index, including data from December 6, 2020 to January 2, 2021, is now available. 

Global travel performance improved slightly toward the end of November and during the first half of December, but lost all the gains after and ended at 42% of the performance during the last week of 2019.

In this final highlights report of 2020, we look back at the entire year and capture the major trends that have shaped the travel industry during this pandemic year.
