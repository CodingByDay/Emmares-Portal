So, Apple had a thing yesterday. A big thing.

It was the WWDC (Worldwide Developer Conference) keynote, where the company announced a bunch of new stuff—iOS 15, iPad OS 15, macOS Monterey, watchOS 8, new features for Health, a bunch of privacy stuff, and … well, you get the idea. The good news? We have all the details down below.

Or, if you’d prefer a quick look at everything, we have that, too. And later today, we’ll outline every device that’ll get the new OS versions, so you’ll know if your stuff made the cut. You can either hit the site later or just catch all the goodies in tomorrow’s newsletter. Totally your call.

Other highlights: 

  • Amazon’s having a big ol’ sale on Roku devices. Call it a Father’s Day sale. Or don’t, it doesn’t really matter.
  • Jeff Bezos is going to launch himself into space. Maybe he’ll come back, maybe not.
  • Samsung showed off a new stretchable OLED display that could be very useful for health and fitness tech. The prototype is being tested on a Band-Aid-like heart rate monitor. Neat.
  • Paramount+ is adding over 1,000 new movies to its service. You may even want to watch one of them!
  • … and more!
