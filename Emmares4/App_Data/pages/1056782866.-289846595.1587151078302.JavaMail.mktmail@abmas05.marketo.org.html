Dear TechCrunch Reader,

Every year, founders, investors, and entrepreneurs shaping the future of tech look forward to TechCrunch Disrupt SF. Over 10,000 attendees from all over the world come to form career-making connections and learn from some of the most successful industry leaders.


Due to the uncertainty brought on by COVID-19, we are adapting to bring our premier event to attendees virtually as well as in person, for those who might face travel uncertainties.


For this year’s event, we will be offering remote attendees two types of digital passes that will provide untethered interactive access to the show online: 

  • Disrupt Digital Free Pass

Pass holders will have access to the Disrupt Stage live stream, as well as access to the full slate of Disrupt Stage content via video on demand. 

  • Disrupt Digital Pro Pass

Pro Pass holders will have access to all the programming on the primary stages (Main, Extra Crunch, Q&A) as well as a substantial amount of real-time networking opportunities with fellow attendees, founders in Startup Alley, interactive Q&A sessions, and TC’s important exhibitors and partners. 


Over the coming weeks, we will be sharing with you the specifics about how sponsorship partners can activate around our new digital offerings. 

We will keep you updated as we continue to navigate new challenges brought on by COVID-19 (you can always check for the latest info here). 


Joey Hinson

Director of Operations
