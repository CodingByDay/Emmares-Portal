Happy Thursday. One of my favorite Google Assistant features is “find my phone.” If I misplace my phone (or it gets lost in the couch, which happens regularly), I can shout to one of my many Assistant displays and ask it to find my phone. Up until now, however, this only worked on Android phones.

Assistant recently got some new tricks, including the ability to find (and ring) your misplaced iPhone. It’s opt-in for now, so make sure to do that if you want to use the feature. Once you’re finished, you can get Assistant to order takeout for you. Nice.
