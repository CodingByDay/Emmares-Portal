Dear customer!
 
In autumn, Vienna has a magic all of its own: nature glows in beautiful colours and the waning summer heat inspires you to get out and about, walk in the footsteps of the Habsburgs, stroll through rustling leaves, dine in vineyard restaurants or explore the “Naschmarkt” – pleasures for all the senses.
 
 
 
   
 
 
 
 
 
  Vienna Wine Hikes  
 
  Where better to have a walk than in the vineyards around the city? Lush greenery that explodes into a firework of different colours in autumn, the scent of warm earth and sun-drenched grapes… Cheers!
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  The Vienna Naschmarkt  
 
  Where gourmets feel at home! Here you’ll find exquisite delicacies from all over the world – a true paradise for food lovers. The only drawback: your eyes may be too big for your stomach.
