Dear Reader,

Yesterday marked the official start of the Atlantic hurricane season. Forecasters expect it to be an active one, though we can’t know ahead of time how many storms might hit the U.S. There are indications, though, that global warming will make hurricanes bigger, stronger and longer-lasting, as my colleague Mark Fischetti discussed with climate scientist Kevin Trenberth in 2018.

Andrea Thompson, Associate Editor, Sustainability
