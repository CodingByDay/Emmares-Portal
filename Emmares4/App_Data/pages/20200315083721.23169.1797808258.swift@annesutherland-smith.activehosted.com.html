Japan Travel Planning - COVID-19 Updates

Reader, as you will be aware, the spread of the COVID-19 virus has seriously disrupted international travel, and has also had a serious impact in Japan.

To help our readers work out what they should do, and to keep them updated on the latest information about Japan, we have set up a dedicated Facebook Group called Japan Travel Planning - COVID-19 Discussion Group. 

In this group we are regularly posting the latest information, and also group members are discussing what is happening in terms of making decisions on whether to proceed or cancel travel, as well as the experiences they are having with various airlines, insurance, accommodation and many more tourism providers.

We invite you to join this group if you are also trying to track this issue, and if you prefer not to read about it the easy option is to NOT join this group!  The main JTP group is still running for more general travel questions for Japan.
