We want to tell you about the changes we are making to this year's Skift Forum Europe on June 30.

In light of global health concerns due to the coronavirus, we have decided to convert Skift Forum Europe into an online event.

Skift Forum Europe 2020 will now take place via an online video platform on June 30, as a fully remote event.

The global Covid-19 crisis decimating the travel industry is presenting steep challenges for Europe, the world’s most strategic tourism destination and its unique tourism economies. As we enter a new phase of recovery planning, an urgent need exists for collaboration between leaders as well as a clear view of the best ideas in response to this staggering event.

 

Skift Forum Europe will present frank conversations on what Europe needs now to find its path forward, rebuild its operations, and create resilience for the future.
