G2E Asia @ the Philippines Product Awards and Gala Dinner
 
The Product Awards and Gala Dinner will take place Tuesday, December 3. The awards will recognize the most popular products and services amongst this year's exhibitors. All registered visitors will be eligible to vote.

Segmented Industry Networking Events
 
Segmented Industry Networking Events will comprise a series of small, interactive, and industry-specific gatherings. This year’ s offerings include table game and slots networking.

The Gaming Executives’ Reception is an invitation-only cocktail event for the industry’s most prominent stakeholders, offering exclusive networking opportunities and brand exposure at the highest level.

The Conference Program, led by high-level industry leaders and influencers, will provide updates on regional developments and practical, cost-effective insights relevant to the challenges facing Asian operators today.
