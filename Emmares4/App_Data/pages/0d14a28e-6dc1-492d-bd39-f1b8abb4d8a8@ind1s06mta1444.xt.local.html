Given the rapid pace of progress in AI, many predict that machine minds could advance to human-level intelligence within the next few decades – and from there quickly outpace us.

In this talk, philosopher and AI specialist, Susan Schneider looks both outwards and inwards to ask what that means for the future of the mind, arguing it is inevitable that AI will take intelligence in new directions.

But will AI minds necessarily be conscious – and how will we find out? And in playing with tools such as mind, consciousness and the self that we don’t yet fully understand, what dangers are we creating for ourselves?
