Changes to keep you safe in stores

Digital deals

Ten at a time

To do our part to limit the spread of COVID-19 and follow guidelines from the CDC and WHO, we are limiting customers in our stores to 10 at any given time. AutoZoners will be maintaining social distance from customers in the store and parking lot, and we ask that you do the same.

Mobile payment available

Checkout card readers now accept Google Pay, Apple Pay, and Samsung Pay to complete contactless transactions.
