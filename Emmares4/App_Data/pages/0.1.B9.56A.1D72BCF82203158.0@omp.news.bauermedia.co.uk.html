Welcome to the CAR newsletter
It’s a great way to keep in touch with what’s happening in the world of CAR – in print, online, and on multiple digital platforms.

Through the newsletter you’ll keep ahead of the curve with the latest expert news and reviews on the new cars that matter. And you’ll get a taste of some of the rich and stimulating content from the magazine, with its world-class mix of writing, photography and design.

CAR is first to the new cars that matter, and has unrivalled access to the individuals shaping the automotive world, from world champions to Silicon Valley disrupters.

Whether it’s tackling the highest pass in the Alps in a new Ferrari or buying an old Fiat on the internet and driving it home from Italy, CAR is home to the world’s best drive stories. And it’s the only car magazine with a dedicated technology section, guiding readers through this fascinating period of unrivalled technological change. Enjoy the newsletter – and enjoy CAR. Ben Miller, editor
