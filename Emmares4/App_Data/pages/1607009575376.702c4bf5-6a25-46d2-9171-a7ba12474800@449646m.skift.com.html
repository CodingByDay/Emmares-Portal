We’re publishing a new Skift Research Report: The State of Corporate T&E 2021: The Path to Recovery. Business travel is at a key crossroads. What will the sector look like in 2021? This new report, which analyzes the results of a survey of several hundred business travelers and travel managers around the world, offers predictions and forecasts for how business travel will evolve in the years ahead.

2020 has created new questions for road warriors and company decision makers. When will business travel start to return? What role will the function serve in corporate culture as it evolves for a post-pandemic world. And what features and policies will a best-in-class travel program offer in 2021 and beyond? 

Download Now

In this report, you'll find:

  • Predictions for when the business travel sector will recover from the downturn caused by Covid-19

  • An analysis of how business travel habits and preferences will evolve as a result of the changes taking place in 2020

  • A review of the results of a survey distributed to business travelers and business travel decision makers about their evolving corporate travel needs

  • Interviews with corporate travel leaders about their predictions for business travel in 2021 and beyond
