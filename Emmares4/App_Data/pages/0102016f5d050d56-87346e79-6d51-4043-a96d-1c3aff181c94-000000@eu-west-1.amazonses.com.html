Before I tell you about our latest FREE magazine offering for everyone, I wanted to firstly wish you all a Happy New Year and then slip straight into some big changes for Total Football Analysis in 2020!

I'm the first to admit that as wonderful as our magazine is, the buying/access/reading process isn't always as smooth as I would have liked for people - so we are taking some big steps to improve that as of this very moment.

Instead of receiving the magazines via email each month, you will now be able to read them LIVE on the website or, if you prefer, download them directly from the site depending on what subscription level you have.

That's right - no more "I haven't received my magazine and it's not in junk mail" emails will need to be sent.

Instead, for the next few weeks I am expecting to be inundated with "it's saying I need to subscribe" - we are expecting a few teething problems along the way as we are basically merging two systems behind the scenes that do not talk to each other, trust me it has made the festive season far less fun than it was meant to be but it is worth it!

We now have the following subscription levels for you:

FREE: By simply resubscribing to the email list (legally, I am not allowed to just transfer you over apparently), you will get access to four free articles a month PLUS the TFA2020 magazine

BITESIZE: The BiteSize subscription gives you access to only the BiteSize magazine and all the back issues of BiteSize magazines - not any of the web content or full magazines. This is important, please read it again!

ALL-ACCESS: As the name would suggest, this gives you access to everything. That's every single magazine we have released, all future magazines for the length of your subscription plus all the content that goes out on the website (120+ a month plus an archive of well over 2000 now).

So that's pretty cool, right?

The first question that probably pops into your head is 'I already have a monthly magazine subscription or a monthly website subscription - what does that mean for me?'

Well, Happy New Year as we have just upgraded you to All-Access!

That's right, if you have any of the old subscription levels currently active we have upgraded you and you'll be able to continue renewing at the old price and get a lot more. Bargain.

As I said, there are going to be lots of teething problems that need ironing out so please do email me and I will get back to you as quickly as I can with the fixes.

We are in the process of uploading all the TFA magazines to date into the new system so they will be available to everyone on All-Access passes, please bear with me.
