Dear Reader,

The ongoing toll of COVID deaths has put many people at risk of prolonged grief disorder, a condition that makes daily functioning almost impossible. Next, we asked our Scientific American colleagues how they're making decisions about masks in light of the CDC's new guidance for vaccinated people. And fungi are the latest deadly pathogen threat.
