New Skift Research — Skift Recovery Index: February 2021 Highlights

The latest report of the Skift Recovery Index, including data from January 31 to February 27 2021, is now available. 

After being stagnant for six months, there finally came some positive news. Global travel index score for February jumped four percentage points from January and approached 50, meaning that the travel industry’s holistic performance was at 50% compared to February 2019.

In this February report, we delve into how new coronavirus case numbers are impacting travel performance but vaccination rollouts have yet to show impact. We also present a few country highlights.
