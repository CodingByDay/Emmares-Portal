Happy Friday! How often do you get calls from numbers you don’t recognize? For me, it happens pretty often. But I always answer because I never know when it’s going to be something important. Thanks to a new feature that Google is testing in its Phone app, however, there may be some saving grace for those calls because it could start automatically recording calls from unknown numbers. While details are still scant at the moment, this could ultimately give users more power to fight back if they get duped by scammers.
