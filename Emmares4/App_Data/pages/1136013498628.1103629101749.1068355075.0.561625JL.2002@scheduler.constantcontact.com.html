THE FIRST AND ONLY SCHOOL OF ITS KIND CREATED IN A HOLLYWOOD STUDIO
We have some great resources for high school filmmakers! Hussian College In Studio Los Angeles is offering a free monthly film forum the last Wednesday of every month to guide high school students in their ambition to create films and pursue a professional career. The next online event is 4/28, RSVP HERE or call 1 (800) 762-1993.

HCLA was named a Top Film School by Variety in 2019 and 2020 and a top 10 budget- friendly film school by The Hollywood Reporter, and is the first college of its kind created inside of a studio with its state of the art campus permanently housed at Los Angeles Center Studios.

Have a student applying for film college? HCLA permanently waived all of its application fees to support students during Covid-19, and its President’s Essay Scholarship (with awards ranging from $12,000-$20,000 for those eligible) has a deadline approaching on April 25th!
