New Skift Research — U.S. Travel Tracker: May 2021 Highlights

U.S. travel rate rose to 42% in May, even higher than February 2020, before the pandemic hit. Among those who traveled, 44% were for vacationing. The leadup to the summer travel season looks very promising. 

Skift Research's newly released U.S. Travel Tracker May 2021  report highlights new trends in travel incidences, consumer sentiments and future travel intents distilled from our May Travel Tracker survey. We also continue to track remote work trend and its impact on travel. 

What you'll Learn From This Report: 

  • Travel incidences, Jan 2020 – May 2021
  • May travel highlights
  • COVID-19 impacted travel, Oct 2020 – Mar 2021
  • Changing consumer sentiments on the economic outlook, Feb 2020 – Apr 2021
  • Consumer intent for 2021 travel
  • Remote work trend and its impact on travel and mobility 
  • How COVID-19 vaccination might impact travel
