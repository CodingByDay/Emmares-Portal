Navigating the Path Forward for Meetings and Events

In this report, Skift and The Venetian Resort Las Vegas reveal how meeting and event industry stakeholders are dealing with the Covid-19 crisis. They share learnings, best practices, and innovations that can enlighten businesses, meeting planners, and venues alike.

Download Now

In this report, you'll find:

  • Laying out the case for the long-term staying power of live events

  • In-depth interviews with experts from across the meetings and events sector about how to navigate the logistical challenges of hosting events in the era of Covid-19

  • How event organizers around the world are addressing issues such as attendee health and safety and hybrid events

  • An assessment of how live events may evolve in the future
