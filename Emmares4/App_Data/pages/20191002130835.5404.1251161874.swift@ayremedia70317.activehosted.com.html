The Story

Bangladesh’s Rapid Action Battalion (RAB) detained Salim Prodhan, an alleged ‘online gambling kingpin’ who was trying to leave the country on a flight to Thailand. Media reports did not indicate Salim’s connection to the underground casinos that were the subject of the ongoing crackdown in the South Asian country. But The Dhaka Tribune did report that Salim is chairman of the Prodhan Group, a Dhaka-based conglomerate which included an online gambling company called P24 Gaming. P24 Gaming has since been taken offline.
