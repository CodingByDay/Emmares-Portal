When it comes to event technology, consolidation seems to be the name of the game. As tech is deployed more readily by meeting planners, companies are starting to get larger, either through mergers or acquisitions. Cvent is an example of a company that has grown massively from acquiring small businesses.

Now the Australian events industry is starting to see something similar. Meeting booking platforms Spare Workspace and Venuemob recently announced a merger, making them the biggest event booking company in the country. Combined under the name Venuemob, the company spans thousands of event spaces, from the very small to the very large.

Jake Dimarco, CEO of Venuemob, talked to Skift about the newly merged company's plans to bring instant booking to large events and the possibility of expanding across Asia-Pacific.
