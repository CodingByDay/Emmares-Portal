Quick Tip: Keep a Whiteboard Handy Post-Ride to Record Needed Maintenance

 

I have a white board by my work bench. When I finish my ride I write down anything that needs to be done to the bike. I don’t usually feel like working on it right after a ride and will only remember it right before the next ride if I don’t write it down.
