Need a bit of novelty to get through the day? Here’s a bit of May 4th trivia for you. Although he didn’t coin the phrase, when British Parilimentarian Harry Cohen made a pun while discussing defense spending about how May 4th, should be National Star Wars Day because you can say “May the fourth be with you.” he set a slow-rolling ball in motion. The legislators didn’t exactly jump on that as a pressing issue, but the pun appeared widely in the press at the time and the idea stuck. Today May 4th is recognized by not just Star Wars fans but officially by Lucasfilm and Disney too.
