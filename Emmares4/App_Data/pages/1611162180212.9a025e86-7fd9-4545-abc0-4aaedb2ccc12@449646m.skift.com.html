New Skift Research — U.S. Travel Tracker December 2020: Further Slip

Travel’s dark winter continues. Despite the long stretch of holidays between Christmas and the New Year, only 28% of Americans travelled in December 2020, dipping further from 32% in November.

However, with the promise of the vaccinations and a new President who has already outlined an aggressive plan to battle the pandemic, Americans seem to be optimistic of their travel plans in 2021. Of the Americans we surveyed in early January, 70% expressed interest in travelling in 2021.

Skift Research's newly released U.S. Travel Tracker December 2020  report highlights new trends in travel incidences, consumer sentiments and future travel intents distilled from our December Travel Tracker survey.

What You’ll Learn From This Report:

  • Travel incidences, Jan – Dec 2020
  • December travel highlights
  • COVID-19 impacted travel, Jul – Dec 2020
  • Changing consumer sentiments on the economic outlook, Jul 2020 – Jan 2021
  • Consumer intent on 2021 travel
