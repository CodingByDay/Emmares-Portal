How COVID-19 is Upending Venture Capital Deals

Join us for a live video Q&A to discuss the turmoil in venture capital as a result of COVID-19. We’ll discuss how the VC community is responding to the economic impact, how companies can get through the tough times ahead and financing in a downturn.

About the speakers:
  • Arjun Sethi is a Co-Founder and Partner at Tribe Capital. Tribe Capital’s investments include SFOX, Applied Intuition, Cover, Front, Prodigy, Carta, Bolt, Patch, Homes, Teampay, Wanderjaunt, Relativity, Humi, Instabase, Huckleberry and Certn.
  • Elizabeth Yin is a Co-Founder and Partner at Hustle Fund. Elizabeth is also a former partner at 500 Startups and an ex-Googler. Hustle Fund focuses on funding pre-seed software startups.
  • Peter Hébert is a Co-Founder and Partner at Lux Capital. Lux Capital's investments include Avail, Bright Machines, Everspin Technologies (NASDAQ: MRAM), Flex Logix, Halo Neuroscience, Matterport, Ripcord, Transphorm, and Vium.
  • Jeff Richards is a Partner at GGV Capital. GGV's past and present portfolio companies include Affirm, Airbnb, Alibaba, ByteDance, Didi Chuxing, Grab, HashiCorp, Opendoor, Peloton, Poshmark, Slack, Square and more.
  • Zoë Bernard is a reporter for The Information, covering venture capital from The Information’s New York bureau. Previously, she was a reporter at Business Insider, where she broke stories on firms like Social Capital and Kleiner Perkins, as well as news on several high profile funding rounds.
  • Kate Clark is a reporter for The Information, covering venture capital and startups from The Information's San Francisco headquarters. Previously, she was a reporter at TechCrunch, where she broke stories on the startup industry.
  • Jessica Lessin founded The Information in 2013 to build the next, leading business news publication. Since then, The Information has become essential reading for hundreds of thousands of professionals across industries for its in-depth, hype-free reporting about technology and the industries it is disrupting.
