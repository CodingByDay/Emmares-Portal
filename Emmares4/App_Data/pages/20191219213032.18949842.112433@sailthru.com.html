Maybe you noticed it this morning as you peeled back the cardboard door on your advent calendar and snatched up the gooey chocolate morsel within, but there are just a few days left before Hanukkah, and just a couple more after that before Christmas Day. And then maybe, as you twirled the nugget of dark chocolate around your mouth and noted how its powerful sweetness is perfectly complemented by a delicate tinge of bitterness on the tongue, you remembered that you haven't yet bought any gifts. No gifts!? How! And just think, you have but mere hours to rectify this oversight. What to do?

Turn to WIRED, of course. Our experts have rounded up some truly great suggestions for last-minute shopping. Fresh hardware for your friends, fun toys for the kids, and maybe a little something special for yourself while you're at it. See our guide to last-minute gifts that will save you this holiday season.

Also this week, we tested the latest AeroPress coffee maker, as well as some cappuccino and latte machines for the home. Because nothing goes with chocolate like a good cuppa joe.

Happy holidays!
