The Final one: Star Wars: Episode IX – The Rise Of Skywalker Exclusive

Nine movies. Three trilogies. One galaxy far, far away. The final episode of Star Wars’ central Skywalker Saga is just a matter of months away – and in a major world exclusive, *Empire* has the first word on *Star Wars: Episode IX – The Rise Of Skywalker*, which brings JJ Abrams back into the writer-director’s chair and concludes an epic family saga that began way back in 1977.

In the November 2019 issue, *Empire* speaks to Abrams, co-writer Chris Terrio, and franchise boss Kathleen Kennedy, to talk the momentous task of bringing the saga home, following up *The Last Jedi*, and tying back into all three eras of the story.

The magazine hits the newsstand today and comes in two cover variants, each adorned by one of the sequel trilogy’s Force-sensitive leads – Adam Driver’s Kylo Ren, or Ben Solo, back in his signature helmet (now with rupturing red cracks) and wielding his crossguard lightsaber, and Daisy Ridley’s Rey, bearing the iconic Skywalker lightsaber that has recurred through the entire trilogy of trilogies. Want both covers? Visit your nearest retailer while stocks last.
