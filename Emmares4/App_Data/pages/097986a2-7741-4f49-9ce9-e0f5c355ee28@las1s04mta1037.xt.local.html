Amsterdam
Delve into the famous art scene with an unmissable trip to Van Gogh Museum or join in the festivities and paint the town orange on King's Day! As the night draws in, the city comes alive with many lively bars; perfect to sample the buzzing music scene… Amsterdam is yours to explore!
Take me to Amsterdam 
 
Budapest
The capital of Hungary is growing in popularity, and with good reason… Explore the city's magnificent architecture, a real feast for the eyes! Or why not attend the annual Budapest spring festival for a burst of culture. Still looking for more to do? With outdoor markets, the famous bathhouses and the many parks in the city - there is a little something for everyone!
