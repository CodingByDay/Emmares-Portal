Editor's Note

The travel industry has not seen this kind of global disruption since the financial crisis of 2008. Each day, the spread of coronavirus brings new uncertainty. Since news of the virus first broke wide out of Wuhan in early January, Skift has been on top of the story from every angle that is important to you. To make that coverage more complete, we have created a central page — click here — where you can follow our latest analysis and commentary throughout the day. Be safe.

— Tom Lowry, Editor-in-Chief
