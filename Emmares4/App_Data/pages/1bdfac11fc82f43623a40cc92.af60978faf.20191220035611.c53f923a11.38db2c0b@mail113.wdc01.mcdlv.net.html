THE LATEST
Game On For Jake Kasdan With Jumanji: The Next Level
He struck box office gold with his popular reboot of a 1995 Robin Williams starrer with Jumanji: Welcome To The Jungle, and now director Jake Kasdan kicks it up a notch with the sequel Jumanji: The Next Level.  

Hirokazu Kore-Eda : The Truth

Japanese director Hirokazu Kore-Eda (The Shoplifters) crafts a surprisingly, distinctly French drama with The Truth, starring Catherine Deneuve.
Nicholas Hammond In Hollywood
The veteran star of TV's The Amazing Spider-Man and The Sound Of Music dials it up beautifully in Tarantino's Once Upon A Time In Hollywood.

The Forecast For 2020

There have been massive shifts on the cinematic landscape over the last few years, and in this special feature, we look at what's in store for 2020.
Top 25 Films Of The Decade
As the decade comes to a close, film critic CJ Johnson runs through his list of the best films of the last ten years. The arguments start...now! 

Bonnie Ferguson: Going Places

With roles in the upcoming Just Ruby and Moonrock For Monday, young Australian actress Bonnie Ferguson is headed for big things indeed.
Tom Cullen: Pink Wall
A seasoned actor with roles in the acclaimed Weekend and TV's Downton Abbey and Knightfall, Tom Cullen now turns director with Pink Wall.
