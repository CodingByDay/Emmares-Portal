Yet another big retail weekend is here! Presidents' Day is this Monday, and whether you have the day off or not, there are plenty of great deals out there to put you in a celebratory mood. It's not just mattresses that are discounted, but also robot vacuums, headphones, tablets, and a whole lot more.

It's also been a busy week for product launches. Samsung's big Galaxy Unpacked event in San Francisco has come and gone, and we got a first look at the Galaxy S20, S20 Plus, S20 Ultra, and the new Galaxy Z Flip foldable phone. (How many is too many phones?) Oh, Samsung also introduced a new pair of earbuds, the Galaxy Buds Plus. In other news, WIRED's Steven Levy gave us a glimpse of some pages from a now-destroyed journal by CEO Mark Zuckerberg, which lays out his ideas and ambitions for Facebook in its early days. We also spoke to engineer Justine Haupt on a handheld rotary cell phone she designed, which became so popular it crashed her website.

If you're looking for more of our recommendations, we recently updated our best sound machines guide so you can sleep easier, and we have a new guide on the best towel warmers. For more roundups, reviews, and deals, read on.
