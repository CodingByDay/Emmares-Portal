Republicans crash impeachment probe

Around two dozen House Republicans stormed a secure room in the U.S. Capitol Wednesday where the top Pentagon official in charge of Ukraine policy was testifying in the impeachment inquiry. The stunt was reportedly approved by President Trump as a protest of Democrats' frequent closed-door hearings in the probe and meant to stall witness testimony from Laura Cooper on delays in U.S. military aid to Ukraine. It's the second time GOP members tried to disrupt an impeachment deposition, and it comes a day after former Ambassador to Ukraine William Taylor gave what may be the most damaging revelations in the investigation yet. 

More:

  • Impeachment proceedings have historically been held behind closed doors so "witnesses cannot coordinate their testimony with one another," Rep. Adam Schiff said last week, pledging that interviews would ultimately be made public.

This is not normal

A crisis of sex ratios. Female baby sea turtles outnumber males 116 to 1 due to climate change, scientists say, because higher temperatures have led to warmer sands, which are more likely to produce female hatchlings.
