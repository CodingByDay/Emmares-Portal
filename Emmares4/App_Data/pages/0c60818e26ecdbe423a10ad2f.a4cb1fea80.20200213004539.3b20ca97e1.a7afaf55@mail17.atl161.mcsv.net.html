Students at Colorado State University will be able to major in marijuana starting this fall. 

 

To be blunt, it’s about time.

 

The school expects the budding program to be in high demand, and after weeding out unqualified applicants, they expect to have 60 supherb students rolling up for classes after 4 years. 

The Cannabis Biology and Chemistry program will consist of 2 tracks: a “natural products” one focused on biology, and an “analytical” one focused on chemistry.

Pandemic chatter

 

With coronavirus fears continuing to grow, are public company execs talking about it during earnings calls? 

So far, not yet — there have been just 6 mentions of "coronavirus" on Q1'20 earnings calls. Many have not happened yet, so we expect this will climb.

 

Even the term "pandemic" hasn’t come up a great deal over time on earnings calls.  

 

As we look back to earnings calls from 2008 using our earnings transcript search engine, we see a peak in Q3'09 (talk of H1N1, aka swine flu), but after that, discussions mentioning the word "pandemic" have generally stayed under 10 per quarter. 


Novavax, Becton Dickinson, and Aethlon Medical mention the term "pandemic" the most. You can see the top 10 here after logging into CB Insights.
