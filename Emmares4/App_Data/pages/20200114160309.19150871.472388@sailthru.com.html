Teriyaki Beef Bowl

Stick to your resolutions!

Busy weeknights are the arch enemy of New Year’s resolutions, but never fear: this easy teriyaki beef bowl is delicious, quick, and (most importantly) healthy. No broken resolutions here!

 

The recipe starts like all great stir fries do: By sizzling up some garlic, ginger, and scallions. Then add the Kroger® Beef Shaved Steak, allow it to brown, then pump up the flavor with soy sauce and rice vinegar. The vegetables couldn't be easier. Just break out your trusty Kroger® Teriyaki Stir Fry Kit and sauté until softened. Add in your beef, toss with the sauce packet from the kit, and stir to combine. To complete your meal, all you need is a bowl of fresh, warm rice.

 

And there you have it. In no time at all, you've got dinner on the table and it's packed with flavor and nutrients. You're all set to stay on track with your resolution!
