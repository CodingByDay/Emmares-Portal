Yesterday Facebook announced that it’s acquiring New York-based neurotech startup CTRL-labs for between $500M and $1B. 

The startup is working on tech that would let people control computers with their brains. Facebook’s VP of AR/VR says the company plans to use CTRL-labs’ technology to create a wristband that will translate people’s neurological signals into digital signals that can control their devices.
