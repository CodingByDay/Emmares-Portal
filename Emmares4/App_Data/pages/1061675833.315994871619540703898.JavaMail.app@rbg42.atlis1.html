Researchers are eager to eavesdrop on the natural world. These soundscapes can reveal population sizes, activity patterns, and other details about local animal populations—but extracting that information from hours of recordings is extremely time consuming. So humans are developing machine-learning algorithms to do the listening for them.

QUOTE OF THE DAY

The rover successfully used its MOXIE instrument to generate oxygen from the thin, carbon dioxide-dominated Martian atmosphere for the first time, demonstrating technology that could both help astronauts breathe and help propel the rockets that get them back home to Earth."

Mike Wall, Space.com
