INVITE FRIENDS
Hopefully on just a short-term basis
 

The Story

Aquis Entertainment is about to take a major hit as authorities in Australia have forced casinos to temporarily shut down due to the Coronavirus pandemic. The casino is expected to let go about 90% of its workforce, at least on the short-term. In a filing with the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX), Aquis explains, "The Casino Canberra operations ceased prior to 12 noon on Monday 23 March 2020. As a result, the Company has made the very difficult but unavoidable decision to stand down approximately 90% of our 235 employees." Aquis have not yet specified whether or not it plans on helping its furloughed employees, as has been the case with some other casino operators forced into temporary hibernation.
