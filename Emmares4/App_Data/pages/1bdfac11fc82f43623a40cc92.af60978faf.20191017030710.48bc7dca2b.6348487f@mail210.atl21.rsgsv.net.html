Ruben Fleischer Revives The Undead With Zombieland: Double Tap
It's been ten long years - and a lot of other on-screen zombies - since Ruben Fleischer gave the undead genre a thorough livening up with the much loved Zombieland, and now he's got the band back together for the highly anticipated sequel Zombieland: Double Tap.

Angus Watts Goes Bush With Locusts

Producer and screenwriter Angus Watts takes us behind the scenes of his slick, snaking and highly inventive Aussie outback thriller Locusts. 
Alexandra Nell: Going Places
Exciting young actress on the rise Alexandra Nell has booked roles in the upcoming Netflix thriller Rattlesnake and the short I'm Not Hurting You.

Trouble On Set #2: More BTS Horrors

From wild weather and faulty technology to feuding actors and directors, here are twelve more torrid tales of film sets beset by big-time trouble.
A Short Tribute To Damian Hill
With his final feature film roles in this week's releases Locusts and Slam, we pay a short tribute to the cinematic gifts of the late Damian Hill. 

Slam: Local Poetry On Screen

Director Partho Sen-Gupta details the inspiration and making of his drama Slam, a topical contemporary drama set in Sydney's south-west.
Nicoletta Mantovani: Pavarotti
The widow of the late and much loved opera great is more than happy with Ron Howard's highly entertaining documentary on the master tenor.
