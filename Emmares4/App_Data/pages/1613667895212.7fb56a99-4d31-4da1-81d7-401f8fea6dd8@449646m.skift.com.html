New Skift Research — U.S. Travel Tracker January 2021: Hope Amid Continued Hiatus

U.S. travel rate dipped to 21% in January, marking the second worst month since the pandemic started.

However, with the virus in deeper retreat and the smoother rollout of vaccines, American consumers’ confidence of a better 2021 continues to reign. 

Skift Research's newly released U.S. Travel Tracker January 2021  report highlights new trends in travel incidences, consumer sentiments and future travel intents distilled from our January Travel Tracker survey. We also reveal findings of two new added questions on remote work status and its impact on travel. 

What You’ll Learn From This Report:

  • Travel incidences, Jan 2020 – Jan 2021
  • January travel highlights
  • COVID-19 impacted travel, Aug 2020 – Jan 2021
  • Changing consumer sentiments on the economic outlook, Sep 2020 – Feb 2021
  • Consumer intent on 2021 travel
  • Remote work trend and its impact on travel and mobility
