We’re publishing a new insights deck, in partnership with Cvent: Decoding Intelligent Sales — The Key to Winning Group Business in 2020, that offers a new approach for hotels to tackle the growing challenges of winning and growing their meetings and events business.

Despite forecasts of continued growth in the meetings industry, hoteliers face an uphill road to achieve their group business goals for 2020. Meeting planners’ demands are evolving and new competitors are making the industry more competitive. In order to succeed in this environment, hoteliers will need a more data-driven and efficient way forward to manage their sales processes, allowing them to effectively differentiate themselves from the competition. In this Insights Deck, Skift and Cvent review the benefits of the intelligent sales approach and offer a roadmap for hoteliers to implement this promising new strategy.
