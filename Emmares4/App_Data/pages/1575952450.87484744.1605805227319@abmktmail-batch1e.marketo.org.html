  TC Sessions: Space 2020 — December 16-17  
 
 
Debuting this year on December 16-17, TC Sessions: Space will explore how to make space more affordable and accessible by bringing together investors, thought leaders, and startup founders.

As a TechCrunch partner, you will have the opportunity to sponsor impactful partner sessions, breakouts, expo booths, networking, videos, and many other opportunities that will engage audience members from all over the world. 

Speakers at this event include Lisa Callahan, Vice President & General Manager of Commercial Civil Space, Lockheed Martin Space, Salvatore Tory Bruno, President & CEO, United Launch Alliance, Peter Beck, CEO, CTO, Founder, Rocket Lab, and Payam Banazadeh, Chief Executive Officer, Capella Space.


With our virtual capabilities, TechCrunch will be taking your sponsorship visibility and reach to infinity and beyond with...


  • Up to 15 live interviews with the key founders, investors, technologists, and government leaders.

  • An expo hall for dozens of exhibitors.

  • A startup pitch-off, in which 10 founders pitch a panel of experts at a private virtual event prior to TC Sessions.

  • Curated networking that builds business connections.
