Connect With Us This Week

Over the past few weeks we've been working hard to provide our readers, listeners, and watchers with new ways get the information they need to do their jobs better. That means more live virtual events in more formats than we've ever tried.

We start and end each work week with a livestream: the Skift Airline Weekly team on Monday, and Skift CEO Rafat Ali and a guest every Friday. In between we host an Online Summit devoted to a particular sector of the travel industry featuring executives and leaders, as well as Skift editors. You can also listen to each day's top story on our 3-minute morning podcast.

Can't join one of our events live? We record everything so you can watch or listen to them on demand.

I look forward to having you join us. Thanks.

— Jason Clampet, Chief Product Officer
