New Skift Research — The U.S. Hotel Ownership Landscape in 2021

The hotel owner landscape is broad and diverse. While hitting the entire industry hard, the pandemic has accentuated the strengths and weaknesses of different owner types.

Skift Research’s newly released U.S. Hotel Ownership Landscape in 2021 report takes a deep look into the major ownership types in the context of the pandemic, with specific focus on what owners need to consider in making decisions on whether to affiliate with a franchise or not, what kind of property and customer to target, and how they market, distribute, and operate their properties.

What You’ll Learn From This Report:

  • An overview of the different role and players that make up the hospitality ‘stack’
  • Branded vs. independent U.S. market share
  • A deep dive into the branded hotel owner today including how brand standards are evolving
  • A look into the distribution capabilities of independent vs. branded hotels including channel mixes, how loyalty evolved during the pandemic, and cost structure
  • A deep dive into the relative performance of different brands and independent properties during and after COVID-19 in the U.S.
