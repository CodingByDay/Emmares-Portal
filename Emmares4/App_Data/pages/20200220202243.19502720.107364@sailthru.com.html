Every spring, our attention turns to Samsung, the world's largest smartphone maker, which launches the latest models in its Galaxy line of phones. This year's edition is called the Galaxy S20, and comes in three flavors.

We haven't gotten the chance to test the phones just yet, but Samsung is already offering them for sale. Preorders on the Galaxy S20, the Galaxy S20+, and the Galaxy S20 Ultra start Friday morning at midnight. We've put together a buying guide to help you decide which one of these Sammies is the best match for your needs. So before you drop over $1,000 (and yes, these phones start at a cool grand), be sure to study our expert advice.
