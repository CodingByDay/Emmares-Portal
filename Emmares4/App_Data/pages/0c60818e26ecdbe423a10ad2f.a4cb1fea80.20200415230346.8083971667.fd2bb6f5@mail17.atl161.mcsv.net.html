Asia VC plummets

 

The full effect of Covid-19 on VC investments hasn’t really been felt in the US or Europe. But as a harbinger of things to come, Asia is worth studying.


And per the PwC and CB Insights Q1'20 VC Report, Asia’s VC deal activity dropped 30% very quickly — going from 1,499 deals in Q4’19 to 1,072 in Q1’20.

The true test of US and European venture activity will be seen in Q2’20, but the Asia experience may offer a window into the future.
