Ultimate luxury: S-Class
The Mercedes-Benz S-Class has one simple task - to be the best. If only Audi, Porsche and BMW weren't also aiming for the same position… This week's group test puts our drivers in the lap of luxury as the S-Class faces the 7 Series, Panamera and A8.

Are these leviathans on borrowed time though? We spy the Mercedes EQE and drive the EQS, as our electric future gets ever closer. There's even an environmentally-friendly* Hummer. At least the GT86's replacement isn't electric, yet - scroll down for more.

Plus, we report on life with our long-term EQC and Cayenne Coupe, interview 'Mr Le Mans' Tom Kristensen and DS Automobiles MD Jules Tilstone, and Gavin Green meets a new friend in our Picture of the Week.

Enjoy, and thanks for reading!
* we know... but no exhaust is good, right?
