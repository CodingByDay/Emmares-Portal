GETTING SMARTER AND MORE PROFITABLE IN A POST-COVID WORLD
Download our latest report. Get the report here.

The lead-in to summer 2021 is revealing signs of recovery for the hospitality industry. Global travel demand is at its highest since the beginning of the pandemic, as the industry finds itself at a promising turning point.

After a year of limitations, the impacts have been widespread. How can those in the hospitality industry use this critical moment to build their businesses back to be smarter, better, and more resilient? To gain further insight, Skift and Oracle collaborated on a global research study during spring 2021, focusing on the current thinking from hoteliers and guests on the post-Covid hotel experience. 

IN THIS REPORT:

  • The results of a 2021 global survey of hotel guests and hotel executives on their changing expectations for the hospitality experience
  • An exploration of how hotels can build their businesses back smarter and better following the Covid-19 pandemic
  • A review of emerging non-room hotel revenue opportunities to help supplement variations in room demand
  • A look at how hotel technology can help hotels exceed guest expectations while running their properties more efficiently
  • Analysis of regional differences in hospitality recovery
